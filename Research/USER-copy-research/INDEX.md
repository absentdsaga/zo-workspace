## Audience Profile: Solana Mobile Builders with PMF

**Who they are:**
- Founders building on Saga/Seeker

- Preparing for dApp Store launch OR
- Already launched, dealing with reviews

**What they fear:**
- Bad dApp Store reviews that tank adoption
- Launching with bugs that appear in first 100 users
- Wasting budget on users who don't convert

**What they want:**
- Confident launch
- Real feedback before launch
- Good dApp Store ratings
