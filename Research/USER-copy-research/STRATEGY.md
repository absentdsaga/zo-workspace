# U$ER Website Copy — Deep Research Strategy

**Goal:** Top 0.01% converting copy backed by research on Solana Mobile builder audience with PMF.

---

## Research Synthesis

### 1. Audience Research: Solana Mobile Builders

**Who they are:**
- Founders building dApps for Saga/Seeker devices
- Have PMF (users, traction) but preparing for dApp Store launch OR already launched
- Technical but business-minded

**What they fear (from X research):**
- ❌ Bad dApp Store reviews that destroy early momentum
- ❌ Bugs discovered by first users, not in testing
- ❌ Cold start problem — no users, no reviews, no social proof
- ❌ 1-star reviews from unprepared launches live forever

**What they want:**
- ✅ Confident launch with bugs fixed
- ✅ Good dApp Store ratings from real users
- ✅ Feedback before their first organic user sees the app

**Key X posts that reveal pain:**
- @deplaydotfun: "We launched yesterday and immediately got our first real users. With live feedback and even a few bugs… which we already fixed."
- @ClaimSol_: Begging for reviews publicly: "If you've tried Claim Sol on the Solana Mobile dApp Store, we'd really appreciate an honest review."
- Multiple devs complaining about bugs: transaction failures, app crashes, shop getting stuck

### 2. Conversion Psychology Research

**Key findings:**
- **Pain-point headlines convert 45% better** (HubSpot 2024)
- **PAS Framework** (Problem → Agitate → Solution) is the highest-converting for B2B
- **"What you get" > "What we do"** — lead with outcomes, not features
- **Specific numbers convert** — "120K+ testers" beats "thousands of testers"
- **Social proof near CTA** increases conversion 30%+

### 3. Web3/Crypto Native Research

**What works:**
- Direct language, no corporate fluff
- Show on-chain proof (numbers, dates)
- Don't overpromise (crypto audiences are skeptical)
- Speak their language: USDC, dApp Store, SBT, Genesis
- Trust through transparency: show real results, real clients

### 4. Pricing Psychology Research

**What converts:**
- Anchor high with tiers (don't show $2K option)
- "Default" choice should be middle tier
- Include 1 clear "no-brainer" upgrade reason
- Capture lower-budget leads with "Let's talk" but don't show cheap option

## Strategic Positioning

### The Core Insight

**The problem:** dApp Store reviews are now visible to everyone. Bad first reviews = permanent damage. Developers are literally begging for reviews publicly.

**The fear:** Launch → first users → bugs discovered → 1-star reviews → reputation destroyed.

**The solution:** Test with real users BEFORE launch, get feedback, fix bugs, THEN launch with confidence.

### Messaging Framework

**Headline formula:** [Pain] → [Consequence] → [Solution]

**Instead of:**
> "Real users test your app. You get the truth."

**Use:**
> "Your first dApp Store review shouldn't be a 1-star"

### Voice

- Direct, no fluff
- Crypto-native: USDC, on-chain, SBT, verified
- Confident, not apologetic
- Short sentences, punchy
- No "we're excited to" language

---

## Copy Principles

1. **Lead with the fear** — Make them feel the pain of bad reviews
2. **Show specific proof** — Numbers, names, dates
3. **Use PAS framework** — Problem → Agitate → Solution
4. **Two CTAs** — One for ready buyers, one for researchers
5. **Tester side matters** — This is a marketplace, show both sides
