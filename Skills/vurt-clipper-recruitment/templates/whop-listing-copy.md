## Long-form listing body

### What VURT is

VURT is a vertical drama streaming platform — think ReelShort or DramaBox, but free and ad-supported. Our originals run 60–90 second episodes; we have multiple shows live and more dropping every week.

We pay clippers because views are how VURT makes money. Every qualified view you bring in is real revenue.

### What we want

- Vertical clips, 15–60 seconds
- Dialogue-driven moments — pick the line, build the cut around it
- Captions burned in (auto-captions or manual, your call — must be readable)
- myvurt.com OR @myvurt on-screen for ≥2 seconds
- Hashtag: #VURT + #[ShowName] (e.g., #KarmaInHeels)
- Tag @myvurt in the caption

### What we pay

- **Base:** $[Dioni decides] per approved clip
- **CPM:** $[Dioni decides] per 1,000 verified views, capped at $[cap] per post
- **Milestone bonuses:** $[bonus] at 250K views, $[bonus] at 1M views
- **Top clipper monthly leaderboard:** $[prize] for #1, $[prize] for #2-#5

Payouts via Whop's standard rails (PayPal, ACH, crypto). 24-hour payout buffer per Whop's anti-fraud policy.

### Rules

1. **5,000-view minimum** before any CPM accrues (filters spam)
2. **30%+ average watch-through** required for CPM to count (we check)
3. **50%+ US audience** — submit a screen-record of your TikTok/IG audience analytics with first submission
4. **No bare reposts** of VURT's own clips. Add edit value: different cut, different hook, different overlay
5. **Submit clips within 1 hour of posting** for view-tracking to start
6. **3-day account warmup** before posting on a fresh account (TikTok shadowbans new accounts)
7. **No spoilers** — we publish a per-episode no-spoiler list with the source pack
8. **Captions required**, watermark required, brand-tag required (see "What we want" above)

### Brand safety

- No clipper-side sponsorships overlaid on VURT clips during the program
- No sportsbook / gambling / tobacco / cannabis insertions
- No AI-generated voice clones of our talent
- No deepfake or face-swap edits

### Source content

After approval, you'll get Discord access with:
- A weekly drop of new episodes
- The source-pack URL per show (clipping-cleared cuts, no finale beats)
- Brand-safety guidelines + the no-spoiler list

### How to apply

1. Click "Join campaign"
2. Connect your TikTok / IG / YT Shorts accounts
3. Submit one sample clip (any dialogue-edit work, doesn't have to be VURT)
4. We approve within 48 hours
