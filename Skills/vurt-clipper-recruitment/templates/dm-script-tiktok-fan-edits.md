# DM Script: TikTok Fan-Edit Accounts

**Target pool:** BookTok-romance edit accounts (5K–500K followers), ReelShort/DramaBox actor fan-edit accounts, FilmTok dialogue-edit creators, Wattpad-romance edit accounts.

**Sourcing hashtags:** #BookTok #BookTokRomance #FilmTok #ReelShort #DramaBox #VerticalDrama #ShortDrama (+ specific actor names from ReelShort/DramaBox catalog: Ben Armstrong, Eric Lutz, Cameron Porras, Joseph Purcell, etc.)

**Goal:** Build a hand-curated list of 100 accounts. Convert 20–30 to paid VURT clippers in Tier 1.

---

## DM v1 — Direct paid offer (under 80 words)

> Hey [name] — your edits are great. I run distro at VURT, vertical drama platform. We just launched a paid clipper program — $[X] base per approved clip + CPM bonus on views. Drama IP, dialogue moments, your style fits perfectly. Want a brief? No pressure, just thought of you when I saw your [specific edit reference].

---

## DM v2 — Soft warm-up (use if v1 feels too transactional for the account)

> Hey — your [show name] edit blew up on my fyp. I work on a vertical drama platform called VURT, we have a similar vibe but new originals you probably haven't seen yet. Would you want early access to clip from? I can send a few.

(Then in follow-up DM after they say yes, drop the paid program brief.)

---

## DM v3 — For larger accounts (50K+)

> Hi [name] — Dioni from VURT. We're scoping our first wave of paid creator partnerships and you're at the top of the list — your [actor/show] edits are exactly the dialogue-driven cut work that fits our catalog. We pay [framework — base + CPM]. Open to a 5-min call this week to walk through the brief?

---

## What to include in the response if they ask "what's VURT?"

> Vertical drama platform like ReelShort/DramaBox but free and ad-supported. We make our own originals — currently Karma in Heels, Girl in the Closet, Killer Stepdad with more dropping monthly. ~15K daily users, 152 countries, Big Boy Reps as one of our first co-pros. We're building creator partnerships first instead of going pure paid ads.

---

## Sourcing checklist (per account)

Before DMing, verify:
- [ ] Posts are dialogue-driven, not just visual aesthetic edits
- [ ] Average view counts ≥5K (so they can hit the payout floor)
- [ ] Audience age skews 18–34 (check comment vibe + display name conventions)
- [ ] US/CA-skewed audience if possible (TikTok analytics not visible from outside, infer from comments + featured-in fyp signals)
- [ ] Active in last 14 days
- [ ] No competing brand partnership currently pinned

## What to NOT do

- Don't mass-DM with the same script. TikTok's bulk-DM detection will flag the account and the recipients block you.
- Don't pitch in the comment section of their post. Always DM.
- Don't send screenshots of analytics or "we have 152 countries" without context — comes off as overcompensating for being early-stage.
- Don't promise specific dollar amounts in cold DM — frame as "paid program with base + CPM" and reveal numbers after they engage.
