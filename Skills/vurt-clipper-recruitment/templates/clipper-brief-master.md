## Cut requirements

- **Length:** 15–60 seconds (sweet spot: 18–30s)
- **Aspect ratio:** 9:16 vertical
- **Captions:** Burned in, readable on phone screen, no overlap with bottom safe zone
- **Hook in first 1.5s** — quote, action, reveal, or visual that earns the second-watch
- **End with a question or open loop** — no full payoff, force them to seek the show

---

## Branding requirements

- **myvurt.com** OR **@myvurt** on-screen for ≥2 seconds (anywhere in the clip — top-right is preferred)
- **NO** ReelShort/DramaBox/ShortMax-style watermarks (we are not them; don't visually echo)
- **NO** auto-translate captions in language other than English unless explicitly requested

## Posting cadence (per clipper)

- **Tier 2 (open pool):** max 3 clips/show/week
- **Tier 1 (verified):** max 5 clips/show/week
- **Tier 3 (ambassadors):** unlimited, but quality > quantity expected

---

## Submission rules

- Submit clip URL via Whop / Discord within **1 hour of posting**
- Include screenshot of your TikTok/IG audience analytics on first submission
- View-tracking starts at submission, ends 14 days post-publish

---

## Brand safety

- Clipper may not stack their own brand sponsorships on a VURT clip during this program
- No sportsbook/gambling/tobacco/cannabis insertions
- No AI-generated voice clones of VURT talent
- No deepfake or face-swap edits
- VURT reserves right to demand takedown for any clip violating brand guidelines (paid out clips will not be clawed back)
