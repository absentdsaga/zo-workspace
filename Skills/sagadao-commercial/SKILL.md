## Script Writing Principles

### Format
Scripts should include:
- **VISUAL** direction (what footage/B-roll to use)
- **VO** (voiceover narration)
- **TEXT** overlays / supers
- **MUSIC/SFX** notes
- **PACING** notes (beat, pause, tempo shift)
- Timecode estimates per section

### Tone
- Confident, not corporate
- Culture-first, not sales-first
- Let the footage sell itself
- Energy builds throughout
- The ask comes after the proof

### Structure (for sponsorship sizzle reels)
1. **COLD OPEN** (0:00-0:15) - Hook with energy, best footage, provocation
2. **THE PROBLEM** (0:15-0:30) - Conference booths are dead, logos on lanyards don't convert
3. **THE PROOF** (0:30-1:15) - Abu Dhabi footage, stats, testimonials, who was there
4. **THE UPGRADE** (1:15-1:45) - Miami is bigger, Miami backdrop, dual conference moment
5. **THE OFFER** (1:45-2:15) - What sponsors actually get, how brands live inside the house
6. **THE CLOSE** (2:15-2:30) - Urgency, scarcity, CTA

### Key Lines to Weave In
- "This is not a conference booth."
- "You're not buying impressions. You're buying credibility."
- "Your brand embedded in genuine lifestyle moments, not logos on a banner."
- "Ready to sponsor culture?"
- Stats: 521+ visitors, $1B+ net worth, 7 days, 8 creators
