## PRODUCTION NOTES FOR SKYRISE

### Footage Pull List (Abu Dhabi)
Prioritize footage that shows:
1. **Scale** - Wide shots of the packed house, multiple nights
2. **Quality of attendees** - Anyone recognizable, suits + streetwear mixing
3. **Organic brand moments** - Any sponsor activations that looked natural, not staged
4. **Energy** - Cheers, reactions, people genuinely having fun
5. **Production value** - Confessional room setup, camera rigs, the SkyRise crew working
6. **Intimacy** - Small group conversations, 1-on-1 networking, real connections forming
7. **The villa itself** - Exterior shots, the setup, the transformation from empty to alive

### VO Casting Notes
- Male voice, 28-40 range
- Confident but not aggressive. Think: someone who's already won and is inviting you in
- NOT announcer voice. NOT hype voice. Think documentary narrator meets brand film
- Slight edge. Cool. Like he's telling you a secret, not selling you something
- Reference: the voice energy of a high-end whiskey ad, not a crypto exchange commercial

### Music Reference
- Gesaffelstein "Pursuit" energy for the open
- Moderat "Bad Kingdom" for the proof section
- ODESZA "A Moment Apart" for the Miami reveal
- The track should feel like one continuous build with a satisfying resolve at the close
- No lyrics. Instrumental only. Electronic with cinematic elements.

### Edit Style
- Clean, modern. Not over-designed
- Text supers should feel like the sponsor deck aesthetic (dark, neon accents, futuristic type)
- Color grade: warm/amber for Abu Dhabi footage, teal/cyan for Miami reveal
- Slow motion used sparingly and intentionally (the cheers, the group shot, the close)
- Let shots breathe in the proof section. Don't cut too fast there. The footage sells itself.

### Deliverables
1. **Hero cut** - 2:15-2:30, 16:9, for direct sponsor outreach (email/DM)
2. **Social cut** - 0:60, 9:16, for X/Instagram (teaser, no pricing, drives to DMs)
3. **Micro cuts** - 3x 0:15 clips for paid social / retargeting
4. **Thumbnail** - Static frame from the best group shot with "SagaDAO House Miami" overlay

### Aspect Ratios
- 16:9 landscape (hero)
- 9:16 vertical (social)
- 1:1 square (optional, for LinkedIn)

## APPENDIX: BEAR MARKET POSITIONING NOTES

When this video is sent to prospects, the outreach messaging should lean into bear market psychology:

**Why crypto companies should sponsor in a bear market:**
- Bear markets are when brands are BUILT. The companies visible now own the narrative when the market turns.
- Conference booth CPMs are at their highest, but SagaDAO House offers cultural placement at a fraction of the cost of a Consensus booth ($25K-$100K+).
- The audience at SagaDAO House skews builder/founder/investor, not retail. These are the people who survive bear markets and control capital when the cycle turns.
- Content from the house has long-tail distribution. A branded moment in May 2026 will be circulating through Solana CT for months.
- In a bear market, being associated with culture and community (not just product) is a massive signal of confidence that resonates with the ecosystem.

**Target sponsors to prioritize:**

- Hardware (Solana Mobile / Seeker)
- Cross-chain players looking for Solana exposure
- Stablecoin issuers (Circle/USDC, Tether)
