---

### The idea
ABFF week is the densest concentration of Black film/TV buyers, creators, and press in America. V1 converts the ~2% who will RSVP. V2 has to do the harder job: earn everyone else onto `myvurt.com` and hold them through the 10-video threshold that activates habit.

This isn't an explainer ad. It's a **statement of category**. Every Chinese-backed micro-drama app is selling "bite-sized drama, anywhere." VURT is selling **Vertical Cinema, Built for the Culture** — the thing those apps can't claim and won't try to.

### Why this hero line is correct (grounded in research)
- **Category wedge.** Ten competitors studied (ReelShort, DramaBox, ShortMax, FlexTV, Goodshort, MoboReels, WeTV, Kalos, PineDrama, FlareFlow) all say "bite-sized," "quick fix," "snack-size," "addictive." None say "cinema." None say "culture." VURT can own both words unopposed.
- **Black-media voice native.** "Built for the Culture" follows the pattern of BET+ "Stream Black Culture," Essence "Where Black Women Are Boundless," Kweli "Binge on the Culture." Possessive + definite article. Flat declarative confidence.
- **OOH rules honored.** 6 words, ≤7 ceiling (OAAA). Two declarative beats, no CTA, brand does the calling.
- **Quotable shape.** Can be repeated verbatim at a dinner or on a panel. That's how ABFF audiences carry a brand after the truck drives off.

### Stickiness loop (after the scan)

1. QR → `myvurt.com?utm_source=truck&utm_content=v2-general`
2. Landing page auto-plays a 90-second highlight reel of VURT originals (muted, captions on — matches phone-default behavior).
3. 30s dwell → overlay: "Keep watching → [chapter 2 of Chief Keef/Glo Navy ep 1]." One tap continues.
4. 4th video → modal: "Get notified when new episodes drop" — captures phone (for SMS re-engagement once 10DLC is live; skip for round 1).
5. 10th video → existing VURT sign-up prompt (per AVOD product rule). At this point behavior has already converted; sign-up is just data capture on a stayer.

---

### Why this matches VURT's KPI
Per the AVOD rule — **primary KPI is video views, not sign-ups.** V2.1 is built for that:
- Every scan lands on autoplay.
- Every frame on the landing page points at "watch more."
- Sign-up is deferred to the behavioral-conversion moment (10th video).
- Meta pixel on `myvurt.com` auto-builds a retarget Custom Audience at near-zero cost.

### Variants (for A/B inside V2 rotation)

- **"One show" cuts** — instead of a montage, six separate 15s side spots, one per VURT original. LPMG rotation does the sampling work. Higher production cost, higher per-pass relevance. Best used in combination with the main spot, weighted 70/30 in rotation.
- **"Creator shout" cut** — a VURT creator on-camera says one line (silent, captioned): *"My show is on VURT."* Humanizes the brand. Needs talent release (single face = hero frame = consent required).
- **"Miami block" cut** — opens with "Miami —" and a single show scene shot in or about Miami. Geo-relevance spikes engagement. Keep for Miami week only.
- **"Names on screen" cut** — rapid text-only cuts of on-platform talent names (Kevin Hart, Vivica A. Fox, Trick Daddy, Meagan Good, Rotimi, Lil Duval, Tatyana Ali, Charles S. Dutton). Text-only avoids release issues entirely. Names do the heavy lifting in a festival crowd. Ends on the hero line.
