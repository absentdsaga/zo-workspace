# V2 — "The TV for what TV won't show"
## Always-on VURT awareness + sticky web visits

### The idea
ABFF week is the densest audience of Black film/TV buyers, creators, and press in the country. V1 converts the ~2% who will RSVP; V2 has to do the harder job of earning everyone else into `myvurt.com` and keeping them there long enough to hit the 10-video threshold that activates habit.

The spot is not an explainer. It's a **statement of what VURT plays that other services won't.** The QR is not a logo button — it's a door labeled "more of this."

### Why this isn't a streaming ad
Every SVOD/AVOD ad says "watch anything, anytime." That line is dead. VURT is specific: culture-first, creator-driven, originals + community submissions, free. The creative leads with actual content. If a viewer can't tell within 3 seconds what KIND of show we make, we've built another Netflix ad.

**Why this line wins:**
- "Free, no sign-up, no ads, just culture" is four truths at once. Each is a separate objection to switching streamers. Stating all four in one breath removes four barriers in two seconds.
- "Culture" (not "community") is deliberate per VURT brand language.

### Stickiness loop (after the scan)
1. QR → `myvurt.com?utm_source=truck&utm_content=v2-general`
2. Landing page auto-plays a 90-second highlight reel of VURT originals (muted, captions on, subtitle-first — matches phone audio defaults).
3. After 30 seconds of dwell: overlay appears: "Keep watching → [chapter 2 of Chief Keef / Glo Navy episode 1]." One tap to continue.
4. After the 4th video watched: modal offers "Get notified when new episodes drop" — captures phone (not email) for SMS re-engagement.
5. After the 10th video watched: sign-up prompt (per existing VURT product rule). At this point the user is already converted — sign-up is just data capture on someone who's clearly staying.

---

### Why this matches the "what the user values" rule
Per VURT AVOD rule: **primary KPI is video views, not sign-ups.** V2 is built for that KPI:
- Every scan lands on autoplay.
- Every frame of the landing page points at "watch more."
- Sign-up is deferred to the moment the user has already converted behaviorally.

### Variant ideas (for A/B inside V2 rotation)
- **"One show" cuts** — instead of the montage, six separate 15s side videos, one per VURT original. Let LPMG rotation do the sampling work. Higher production cost, higher relevance per pass.
- **"Creator shout" cut** — a VURT creator on-camera says one line: "My show is on VURT. Scan it." Humanizes the brand. Works only if talent releases are in place.
- **"Miami block" cut** — spot opens with "Miami —" and a single show scene shot in or about Miami. Geo-relevance spikes engagement; keep for Miami week only.
