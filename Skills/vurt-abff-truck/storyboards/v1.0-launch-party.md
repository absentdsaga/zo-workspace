# V1 — "VURT is here"

## VURT Opening Night · Friday May 29, 2026 · Gates Hotel South Beach

### The idea

This is **VURT's coming-out moment in Miami** — t3he night the brand formally lands in front of culture. The truck is not selling "a party." It's announcing that VURT exists, then framing the rooftop as where Miami meets it. The QR is the door. Ground floor = VURT experience, open to walk-ins + invitees. Rooftop = 500-cap invite-only.

**Hero message: "VURT IS HERE."** The party is the answer to *what that means*, not the subject of the ad.

### Why this beats a generic party ad

A static "ROOFTOP FRI 5.29 RSVP HERE" card is just a Miami party — invisible to anyone not already party-hunting. The creative has to earn a 2-second glance by making you wonder *what VURT is* before it asks you to scan. We do that by:

1. **Lead with "VURT IS HERE"** — brand declaration first, event second. The ad works whether you're at the light for 3 seconds or 30.
2. **Frame the rooftop as the opening night** — not an RSVP, a debut. Velvet rope energy, not form fatigue.
3. **Place the QR oversized on the rear face** where cars behind the truck dwell on it for 10–60 seconds at red lights.
4. **Stack the hosts** (Tarik Brooks, Ted Lucas, Hilmon Sorey) above the QR — signals "real night, real people, real brand" in one glance.

### SIDE (16:9 · 1920×1080 video · SILENT — Miami Beach §46-152 bans vehicle audio)

**15-second spot. Pedestrian + sidewalk audience. All meaning carried by type, motion, cuts.**

| Beat | Time | Visual | Type/motion |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | 0.0–1.5s | Black → VURT logo stamp (hard cut, 6 frames) | Logo holds, pulse once |
| 2 | 1.5–2.5s | Black card | "VURT IS HERE" types in, full hold |
| 3 | 2.5–5.5s | Rapid cuts: 4–6 talent/show frames (0.5–0.6s each) from across the VURT slate — Chief Keef, Karma in Heels, Come Back Dad, etc. — intercut with Miami-night texture | Kinetic type: words "THE" · "TV" · "FOR" · "WHAT" · "TV" · "WON'T" · "SHOW" punch in rhythm with cuts, one word per cut |
| 4 | 5.5–8.5s | Title card: "OPENING NIGHT · FRI 5.29" (bold, VURT color on black) | Type types in, 3-frame hold |
| 5 | 8.5–11.0s | Title card: "ROOFTOP · MIAMI BEACH" with small-caps host line below | Hard cut, hold |
| 6 | 11.0–14.0s | QR pulls center-stage with "SCAN TO REQUEST INVITE" under it | QR zooms in, type anchors below |
| 7 | 14.0–15.0s | VURT logo + `myvurt.com/abff` URL | Logo + URL hold for scan-back |

*"Kinetic type" = text that animates with the cuts: letter-by-letter or word-by-word punches, in rhythm with the edit. Not a still caption — it behaves like a cut itself. Keeps a silent 15s from feeling flat.*

Design rules for both faces:

- **No audio.** Silent creative. Do not brief audio.
- **No whites bleeding.** If using light text, use VURT color as fill or a bold black stroke.
- **Font: one bold sans-serif, two weights max.** Skinny fonts vanish on LED.
- **All text legible at 40 ft.**
- **Logo visible in every second of runtime** — no "delay reveal" tricks.
- **No ABFF mention.** VURT is not an official sponsor.
- **Talent cuts are brand texture, not endorsement.** Fast-rotation cuts across many faces are fine without individual OOH releases. Consent is only triggered if a single face becomes the hero of a frame (reads like sponsorship).

### Stickiness loop (after the scan)

**Landing page (**`myvurt.com/abff` **before 5/29):**\
Headline: "VURT IS HERE. Fri 5.29 is our opening night in Miami Beach."\
Split CTA below:

- **\[Request rooftop invite →\]** (primary button, solid VURT color)
- **\[Ground floor walk-in info →\]** (secondary button, outline)

**Rooftop path (requested invite):**

1. Form: first name + phone + email + "how we know you" field.
2. Routes to internal guestlist review (Dioni + team).
3. Instant auto-reply: "Request received. We'll confirm within 48 hours. — VURT"
4. On approval: "You're on the list for VURT Opening Night, Fri 5.29. Address + time sent Wed 5.27. — VURT"
5. Wed 5.27 8 PM: venue + dress code + "bring 1" referral link.
6. Day-of 5.29 3 PM: arrival instructions + map pin + which entrance.
7. Post-event 5.30 11 AM: "Here's what opening night looked like → \[recap on myvurt.com\]".

**Ground-floor path (walk-in):**

1. Info page: address + Fri 5.29 doors open time + "open to public, 21+ with ID."
2. Lightweight email capture: "Tell me when the next VURT thing hits Miami."
3. Day-of 5.29 3 PM email: reminder + "if the rooftop looks full, downstairs is where it's happening."
4. Post-event: add to general VURT newsletter.

Each text/email re-routes back to VURT web/app. That's the whole point.

### Variant ideas (for A/B inside V1 rotation)

- **"500 cap" version** — subline "500 ON THE ROOF · SCAN TO REQUEST" frames scarcity without saying "exclusive."
- **"Quiet" version** — nearly black panel with only "VURT IS HERE" and a flickering QR. Stands out against bright LED spam. Highest aesthetic upside, lowest information density.
- **"Show-first" version** — lead with 1 recognizable show still instead of the word "HERE." Hero line becomes "VURT · OPENING NIGHT FRI 5.29." Works if a specific show is the hook more than the brand declaration.
