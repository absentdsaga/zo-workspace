# DR Bank Investigation — Scotiabank 302639 + SIB Sweep

## Goal

Answer two questions:
1. **Was Scotiabank account 302639 drained after 13 Jun 2021?** If yes, when, how, by whom, and where did the money go.
2. **Did Delio hold accounts at any OTHER DR financial institutions?** (Amarilys's side has told the family "accounts were empty" — that statement is unverified and must be treated as potentially false until confirmed by primary records from the institutions themselves.)

## The legal basis — secreto bancario lifts for heirs

The default is Art. 56 lit. b) of **Ley 183-02 (Ley Monetaria y Financiera)**, modified by Art. 362 of Ley 249-17: banks owe confidentiality to their *clients*. Published SIB and ABA guidance, and DR doctrine, treat that confidentiality as a personal right of the client that **does not survive death**. Heirs who duly accredit their status step into the information rights of the causante. If a bank resists:

- Escalate to the SIB / ProUsuario as supervisor.
- Seek a judicial order (medida conservatoria / ordenanza from the juez de los referimientos).
- File criminal denuncia and request the fiscalía oficie the bank.

SIB's own blog post explaining this: https://www.sb.gob.do/publicaciones/blog-institucional/orientacion-para-herederosas-acceso-a-las-informaciones-bancarias-de-personas-fallecidas/

## Track 1 — Scotiabank DR (account 302639, onward-credit 1058909)

### What to request

**Two documents, requested together in one solicitud:**

1. **Certificación de Balance (del cliente fallecido)** — lists every product/account the decedent held at Scotiabank DR and the balance as of date of death (or current).
2. **Estado de Cuenta histórico + Relación de Movimientos** — transaction-level detail for account 302639 (and onward-credit 1058909), for a defined period: from **13 Jun 2021 through present date**. This is what will show whether it was drained, when, by what mechanism (ATM / POS / teller / transfer / wire), and where the money went.

Also ask for:
- Counter-teller withdrawal slips and the ID captured at the counter for post-13-Jun-2021 withdrawals.
- Any joint signatories, poder holders, or debit card issuance records on file.
- CCTV preservation request (banks hold footage ~90 days; irrelevant for 2021 but preserve for any current activity).

### Who requests it / what they need

Scotiabank DR's published heir requirements (official page: https://do.scotiabank.com/acerca-de-scotiabank/certificaciones/informaciones-requisitos-proceso-cliente-fallecido.html):

- Written solicitud on Scotiabank's template.
- **Original acta de defunción.**
- **Cédula copies** of heir(s) and of the deceased.
- If filed by an apoderado (i.e., the abogado, or Davie on Gisela's behalf from the US): **original Poder de Representación**, notarized and **legalized by the Procuraduría General de la República**. Must specifically authorize requesting balance certification AND historical movements AND (eventually) withdrawing funds. Foreign-signed powers need **apostille** (DR is a Hague member) and **Spanish translation by a traductor judicial**.
- Testamento auténtico (establishes Gisela's heir status as sole heiress — she is Delio's collateral heir by will, not by default succession).

Contact: Scotiabank call center 809-567-7268. The request is handled at the branch where 302639 is booked (likely Santiago) or any branch with the right paperwork.

Practitioner norm for turnaround: **15–30 business days** once the file is complete.

### Phase-2 (release of funds — not today's goal but good to know)

Once you're ready to pull the money into Gisela's hands, Scotiabank will also require:

## Track 2 — SIB / ProUsuario "Consulta de Información Financiera" (sweep ALL banks)

This is the mechanism to discover hidden accounts. It queries every SIB-supervised institution in the DR (all banks, bancos de ahorro y crédito, asociaciones de ahorros y préstamos, corporaciones de crédito) and returns a consolidated certification of every product the causante held anywhere in the system.

- **Official page:** https://prousuario.gob.do/servicios/informacion-financiera
- **Cost:** free.
- **Turnaround:** up to 60 calendar days (Ley 107-13); 10 business days to cure incomplete applications (extendable +5).

**Who can request:** heir, spouse/unión libre, or legal representative with specific POA.

**Documents:**
- Cédula of deceased (or JCE certification if cédula expired at death).
- Acta de defunción (certified by JCE, or foreign-issued with apostille).
- Proof of heir status: birth certificates establishing filiation + **Compulsa del Testamento** (since succession here is testamentary, not by default blood-line) OR a notarial / judicial declaratoria de herederos. For collateral heirs (Gisela as Delio's sister) the testamento is the decisive document.
- If filed by apoderado: **POA notarized and legalized by the Procuraduría General de la República**, specifically authorizing the Consulta de Información Financiera request. ProUsuario publishes a POA template.
- Spanish translations for any foreign documents (traductor judicial).

**Submission options:**
- In person: ProUsuario offices (Santo Domingo HQ, Santiago regional office).
- Email: contacto@prousuario.gob.do
- Phone/WhatsApp: 809-731-3535.

**Scope limit (important):** ProUsuario returns **product and status** (accounts, CDs, loans, credit cards — open/closed/balance). It does NOT give transaction history. For movements you still go bank-by-bank using the Scotiabank procedure (Track 1) for each institution identified.

## Track 3 — If the bank stalls or information is incomplete

If Scotiabank drags or the information conflicts:

- **Administrative escalation:** complaint at ProUsuario (SIB's consumer-protection arm). Scotiabank is a regulated sujeto obligado and responds to ProUsuario pressure.
- **Juez de los referimientos / medida conservatoria:** the abogado can seek a court order (a) freezing any remaining balance, (b) ordering the bank to produce transaction records, and (c) preserving evidence. Referimiento is a fast-track procedure designed for exactly this.
- **Criminal oficio:** once the denuncia penal is filed in Santiago, the fiscalía can oficie Scotiabank for information under the criminal investigation exception — banks comply with fiscalía requests without requiring the full heir-accreditation package.

## UAF — Unidad de Análisis Financiero (financial intelligence)

Can't file with UAF directly — UAF receives ROS (Reportes de Operaciones Sospechosas) from sujetos obligados (banks). But:

1. In the denuncia penal, **request that the fiscalía oficie to UAF** asking for any ROS or historical intelligence tied to account 302639, onward-credit 1058909, or cédula 031-0235285-7 (Amarilys).
2. Have the abogado copy UAF on the denuncia as a "collaboration request."

UAF was created by Ley 155-17 and transmits financial intelligence to the Ministerio Público — this is the pipeline that turns bank records into actionable evidence. Especially relevant because the fund source is a cross-border wire from SSA/US Treasury, which triggers Ley 155-17 lavado de activos analysis.
