## Notes for the abogado

- The exact article of the Ley Monetaria y Financiera for a post-death freeze is something the abogado should confirm per current banking circulares from the Superintendencia de Bancos. The letter can land either way — the request for "retención y no disposición" backed by the succession documents is standard practice regardless.
- Attach the Scotiabank international-transfer form (photo in `evidence/caso-herencia-delio.pdf` pg 4) as exhibit (v). It's the proof the account existed.
- Parallel to this letter, the abogado should also file the **SIB (Superintendencia de Bancos) Certificación de Entidades Financieras** request — that sweeps ALL banks in DR, not just Scotiabank.
