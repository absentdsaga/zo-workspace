# VURT Platform Playbooks
### Last Updated: March 23, 2026

## TikTok (60% of effort)

**Current state:** Handle in cooldown transition from team transfer. ~0 functional presence.
**Goal:** Growth engine. This is where the audience is.
**Cadence:** 3-5 posts/day once handle secured

### Posting Windows (ET)
- Slot 1: 7-9 AM (morning scroll)
- Slot 2: 12-1 PM (lunch break)
- Slot 3: 7-10 PM (prime time, highest Black audience engagement)

### Content Mix
- 50% Clip Baits (peak drama, cut at cliffhanger)
- 20% Culture Commentary (trending topic tie-ins)
- 15% Creator/Cast Spotlights
- 15% Community/UGC (polls, theories)

### Clip Bait Formula
1. Open with most visually/emotionally arresting 2 seconds
2. Drama plays 30-50 seconds
3. CUT at exact peak tension
4. Text overlay: "Watch the full episode on VURT"
5. Pinned comment: "Link in bio"
6. Sound: trending audio when mood-appropriate, original dialogue when scene is strong

### Specs
- Duration: 30-60s sweet spot (up to 3 min)
- Aspect: 9:16
- Hook in first 1.5s
- Text overlays for muted viewing
- No other platform watermarks

## Instagram (25% of effort)

**Current state:** ~334 followers, 21 posts. Best existing channel.
**Goal:** Community hub. Engagement + DMs + broadcast channel.

### Content Types
| Type | Cadence | Purpose |
|------|---------|---------|
| Reels | 1-2/day | Re-edited TikTok clips (adjust safe zones, cover frame for grid) |
| Stories | 5-10/day | Polls, countdowns, BTS, cast takeovers, link stickers |
| Carousels | 2-3/week | Character breakdowns, "5 reasons to binge [title]", creator spotlights |
| Feed Posts | 1/week | Premium brand moments: announcements, press, milestones |
| Broadcast Channel | Launch ASAP | "VURT Insiders" for drop alerts + exclusive content |

### Posting Windows (ET)
- Reels: 11 AM-1 PM or 7-9 PM
- Stories: Spread throughout day (morning, midday, evening)

### Specs
- Reels: 30-60s, 9:16, cover frame matters for grid
- Stories: 15s segments, polls/questions/countdowns overlaid
- Hashtags in caption not overlay
- Different text placement than TikTok (account for IG UI)

## YouTube Shorts (10% of effort)

**Current state:** 7 subs, 4 Shorts. Overtown clip hit 1.1K views = proof of concept.
**Goal:** Discovery engine via search + suggested.

### Strategy
- 1-2 Shorts/day
- Full first episodes as Shorts (free sample model)
- Compilation Shorts: "Best moments from [title]" 58s supercuts
- Let scenes breathe slightly longer than TikTok cuts
- YouTube rewards watch time over engagement bait

### Specs
- Duration: 30-58s (hard cap 60s)
- Aspect: 9:16
- Title card in first 3s
- Subscribe prompt overlay
- Less text-heavy than TikTok

### Posting Windows (ET)
- 12-3 PM or 5-7 PM (YouTube peak)
