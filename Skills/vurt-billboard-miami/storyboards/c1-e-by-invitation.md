# C1-E — "By Invitation" (Opening Night)
## Window: 5/26 – 5/29 · Audience: ABFF industry · Goal: rooftop RSVPs

*Replaces C1-A/B/C/D. All prior C1 archived in Documents/VURT-Billboard-Miami/. Research-rejected reasons:*
- *C1-A/B (type-only "VURT IS HERE"): no FOMO mechanism, no value prop, no event details — board reads as "what?"*
- *C1-C (phone + event): phone-frame on horizontal 840×400 wastes 60% of canvas (Reelshort/DramaBox don't use phone-shapes because their videos are already vertical)*
- *C1-D (4 cofounder names alone): vampire effect — names alone without scaffolding = cognitive load, not FOMO. Names need a frame word ("Hosted by") and ≤30% of visual weight to parse as proof not ask.*

---

## The idea
**The headline does the FOMO. Names support it.**

Industry attendees read "BY INVITATION" + "ROOFTOP" + "5.29" in 2 seconds and either feel "those are my rooms — am I on the list?" or "I should know if my friends are." Tourists feel nothing. That asymmetry is the design (Wallach JCR — exclusion is the connection mechanism for the right reader).

The cofounder names appear smaller, prefaced "Hosted by," doing what they're meant to do: prove the rooftop is real and the room is industry-credible. Not the headline.

### Type spec
- **Hero line 1:** "BY INVITATION." · Inter Bold · ~120 px · #EBEBEB
- **Hero line 2:** "ROOFTOP. 5.29." · Inter Bold · ~120 px · accent color (VURT brand red or warm white — A/B with gfx guy)
- **VURT logo:** top-right, ~80 px tall, ~12% of frame width
- **Event meta line:** Inter Medium · ~28 px · #EBEBEB · "Opening Night · Gates Hotel South Beach · Miami"
- **Cofounder line:** Inter Regular · ~24 px · #999 · prefaced with "Hosted by"
- **CTA:** IBM Plex Mono · ~32 px · accent color · "myvurt.com/abff →"

### Background
Solid #0B0B0B. No gradient. No image. Black panel against Miami's saturated DOOH baseline = contrarian recall +38% (OAAA).

### Optional accent
A single thin vertical line (~3 px wide) accent color, top-to-bottom, separating the hero block from the support block. Adds visual structure without competing with type.

## Why this works (research-anchored)

| Mechanism | How it shows up |
|---|---|
| Specific scarcity (Hodkinson) | "BY INVITATION" — hard gate, not "limited spots" |
| Dated deadline | "5.29" not "soon" |
| Target-specificity / IYKYK (Wallach) | Industry recognizes; tourists don't — by design |
| Social proof | 4 cofounder names as proof line, ≤30% weight, prefaced |
| Single dominant focal point | Hero block is the one thing the eye lands on |
| ≤7 words headline | "BY INVITATION. ROOFTOP. 5.29." = 5 words |
| One CTA | myvurt.com/abff only, no QR (driver-first design) |

---

## What to A/B test with gfx guy

1. **Hero phrase variant:** "BY INVITATION. ROOFTOP. 5.29." vs "INDUSTRY ONLY. FRI 5/29." vs "THE LIST IS CLOSING."
2. **Accent color:** VURT brand red vs warm white vs Miami-coded teal (would need brand-guide check first; default is brand red)
3. **Cofounder line:** all 4 names vs top 3 (Tarik · Ted · Hilmon) — Eric is currently confirmed but the visual gets cleaner with 3
4. **CTA destination:** `myvurt.com/abff` vs `myvurt.com/rsvp`
5. **"Hosted by" vs "Presented by"** — Presented reads more cinematic, Hosted reads more party

## Open before production
- Cofounder greenlight on having names on board (each individually)
- Confirm myvurt.com/abff route ships before 5/26

- Vendor confirm: pedestrian or driver-facing? (decides if QR can be added as secondary)

## Failure modes to avoid
- **Adding QR.** Driver-facing kills it. Only add if vendor confirms pedestrian.
- **Mentioning ABFF.** VURT is not a sponsor.
- **Genre tags.** This is the FOMO board, not the platform-range board.
- **Show stills / talent faces.** Wrong board for it (saved for C2-G).
- **Phone-frame device.** Wrong canvas. Don't squeeze 9:16 into 2.1:1.
