# C2-G — "Single Hero" (Always-On VURT brand)
## Window: 5/30 – 5/31 · Audience: festival lingerers + general Miami · Goal: brand awareness + myvurt.com visits

*Replaces C2-A through C2-F. All prior C2 archived in Documents/VURT-Billboard-Miami/. Research-rejected reasons:*
- *C2-A/B (type-only): no value prop visualized — passerby can't decode "what is VURT?"*
- *C2-C (phone-dominant): phone-shape on 2.1:1 horizontal wastes 60% of canvas*

---

## The idea
**One show. One face. One line. Brand IS the URL.**

This is the Tubi/Netflix/Max pattern proven at scale. For an unknown streaming brand the work is double: introduce the brand AND show why to care. A single dominant talent photograph from VURT's licensed catalog (Eric/Swirl clearance is in place — Rotimi, Vivica A. Fox, Meagan Good, etc. are usable) does both jobs. The face attracts attention. The headline names the platform. The URL closes the loop.

The triptych+culture-tags impulse was right strategically (prove range) — but research is consistent: 840×400 doesn't have the pixel density for it. Range gets proven on the rotation across multiple slot plays, not within a single panel.

## Why this works (research-anchored)

| Mechanism | How it shows up |
|---|---|
| Single focal point | One face, one tagline, one CTA |
| 70% image / 20% type / 10% logo+CTA ratio | Photo dominates left half, type column tight |
| ≤7 words for hero phrase | "Vertical Cinema. Built for the Culture." = 6 words |
| Black contrarian field in saturated Miami DOOH | +38% recall (OAAA) |
| Brand differentiator visible | "ALWAYS FREE TO WATCH" — distinguishes from Reelshort/DramaBox who paywall |
| Culture-fluent talent (Nielsen 2026) | 67% of Black audiences pay more attention to ads reflecting culture |
| Established-brand pattern (Tubi/Netflix) | One show, one face — proven |

Range is proven by *rotation*, not by triptych. Same layout = brand consistency. Different face = "this platform has range." This is how Max ran their rebrand (per research).

## Failure modes to avoid
- **Three faces in one panel.** Mush at 10mm pitch. One face only.
- **Phone-frame mockup overlays.** Tried. Wrong canvas.
- **Culture tag triptych.** Saved for paid social retarget where pixel density allows.
- **App-store badges.** VURT social ops rule: drive to web (myvurt.com), not app stores.
- **QR on driver-facing.** Add only if vendor confirms pedestrian placement.
- **Pure white #FFFFFF.** Glares and blooms on LED — use #EBEBEB.
