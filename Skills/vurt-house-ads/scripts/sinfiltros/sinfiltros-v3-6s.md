# SINFILTROS NUTRITION — Ad Scripts (6-second spec)

Spec: **6s, 1080x1920 vertical, audio-on, non-skippable, clickable end card via VAST.**

## What wins at 6s in wellness gummy DTC (Bloom Nutrition, Goli, Olly, Flo benchmark)

The category-winning beat structure:

- **0–0.5s:** Founder face, direct-to-camera, eye contact, **ONE sentence hook** that names the problem ("My PMS used to ruin my week.") — this is the entire pattern interrupt.
- **0.5–2s:** Bottle reveal in hand, label readable.
- **2–3.5s:** **Mouth shot.** Gummy goes in, real chew sound, real reaction face. (This is Goli's signature beat — the proof frame that makes the category convert.)
- **3.5–5.5s:** B-roll of the benefit — calm morning, skin glow ring-light close-up, hair flip, nail tap. **The "after" beat.**
- **5.5–6.0s:** Pack shot + price/offer or "tap to shop." URL must be readable at arm's length.

**The mistake to avoid:** Over-explaining ingredients (ashwagandha, biotin, KSM-66) in the first 3 seconds. Ingredient education converts at 30s, not 6s. Lead with founder face + named problem + mouth shot proof. Save ingredients for the PDP.

**Second mistake to avoid:** Stock-feeling lifestyle b-roll instead of the founder. **Valentina's 6.5M TikTok is the asset.** If she's not on screen, the spot is invisible.

## What's unique to SinFiltros (the wedge)

Latina-coded wellness without the pastel-girly slop. Real coffee, real mornings, kitchen light. **Bilingual is not optional** — Latina audience is where the demand pool sits, ES variants ship at parity with EN.

Two product lanes:

- **Moodmies** = PMS / cramps. Sharpest wedge. Highest-intent buyer. Lead spot.
- **Glowmies** = skin/hair/nails. Steady-state. Beauty-aisle DR.

## Palette + end card

SinFiltros does not publish a brand color system. Cream / off-white site background. **Pull product accent color from the actual bottle photography per spot:**

- **Moodmies:** pink/coral from the actual Moodmies bottle (extract exact hex from product photo)
- **Glowmies:** powder blue `#C2CAD2` from the actual Glowmies bottle (verified from site photography)
- **Background:** cream / off-white from site (`#F5EFE6` range, matches her photography)
- **Wordmark:** sans, slightly rounded, near-black `#1A1A1A`

End card content (pick one per spot):

- `MOODMIES · for PMS · sinfiltrosnutrition.com`
- `MOODMIES · para los cólicos · sinfiltrosnutrition.com`
- `GLOWMIES · skin · hair · nails · sinfiltrosnutrition.com`

## Spot #1 — "Day One" (Moodmies, EN — founder face hook → bottle → mouth → benefit → end card)

**Wedge:** Hero PMS spot in English. Follows the wellness-gummy winning pattern exactly: founder face + named problem + bottle + mouth + after + end card. Run as the always-on EN workhorse for women's wellness audiences.

**Visual beats (6.0s total):**

- **0.0–0.5s:** **Archival Valentina direct-to-camera**, exasperated/frustrated face, hand to belly. Pulled from her own TikTok feed (with consent). Or fresh phone-selfie if she'll do 60 seconds.
- **0.5–2.0s:** Hand reveals **Moodmies bottle**, label readable, on a real kitchen counter or vanity in soft morning light. Bottle tilts toward camera, one shake.
- **2.0–3.5s:** **Mouth shot.** Gummy goes in. Real chew. Real shrug of "okay, that's better."
- **3.5–5.5s:** **B-roll of her getting on with the day** — walking out the door, sipping coffee, laughing on a call. Pulled from archival or AI-generated lifestyle b-roll. Music kicks here.
- **5.5–6.0s:** End card. `MOODMIES · for PMS · sinfiltrosnutrition.com`. Cream background + Moodmies pink accent.

**Audio:**

- 0.0–0.5s: Her ambient (sigh, soft laugh, room tone).
- 0.5–2.0s: Bottle shake. Soft beat enters.
- 2.0–3.5s: Chew sound.
- 3.5–5.5s: Music bed lifts — warm, mid-tempo, Latina-coded but not novelty (Suno v4 custom-generated).
- 5.5–6.0s: Beat resolves. Clean end.

**VO:** Valentina — **"PMS day one. Moodmies."** (5 words, delivered over 0.0–0.5s + 5.5–6.0s. Phone-recorded in 60 seconds OR ElevenLabs v3 clone with signed consent.)

**On-screen text:**

- 0.0–0.5s: Subtle lower-third — `PMS DAY ONE` (small, off-white, 60% opacity).
- 5.5–6.0s: End card only.

**Production note:** Archival clip must be ≤2s and from Valentina's own posted TikTok or IG. Video guy pulls directly from her public feed — no text-back needed. Editor's job: comb the @valentinasinfiltros feed for the strongest direct-to-camera "ugh, cramps" frame and lock that as the open. Zero generated face — this is the convert beat, must be her real face.

## Spot #3 — "La Rutina" (Moodmies + Glowmies stack — bundle / AOV wedge)

**Wedge:** Brand-world spot. Cross-sells both SKUs in one morning routine. Different rhythm from Spots #1 and #2 — opens on the products together, **not** on founder face. Pattern interrupt = "two wellness bottles on a real Latina-coded counter," which is unfamiliar in feed and sticks. **Bundle wedges are the highest AOV multiplier in early house-ad performance** — every viewer who buys via this spot is worth ~2× a single-SKU buyer. Run as the AOV workhorse alongside Spot #1's PMS-direct workhorse.

**Visual beats (6.0s total):**

- **0.0–1.0s:** **ECU on both bottles** — Moodmies pink + Glowmies powder blue — side-by-side on a vanity / kitchen counter in soft morning light. Real space, not styled. Hand reaches into frame and picks up the Moodmies first. Pattern interrupt is the visual: two bottles, real morning.
- **1.0–2.5s:** **Quick cut to Valentina** (archival, pulled from her public IG/TikTok feed) tipping a Moodmies into her palm, then the Glowmies. Real face, real countertop, no post.
- **2.5–4.0s:** **Mouth shot.** Both gummies in — pink chew, then blue chew. Real "okay, that's the routine" face.
- **4.0–5.5s:** **B-roll — her on with the day.** Coffee, laughing on a call, walking out the door. Last frame holds on a wide of the counter — both bottles still sitting there as she leaves. The "this is what's in her morning" beat.
- **5.5–6.0s:** End card. `MOODMIES + GLOWMIES · la rutina · sinfiltrosnutrition.com`. Cream background, split accent bar — Moodmies pink on the left half, Glowmies powder blue on the right half — to signal both products without a busy lockup.

**Audio:**

- 0.0–1.0s: Soft morning room tone, single warm guitar note.
- 1.0–2.5s: Bottle shake, gummies into palm.
- 2.5–4.0s: Two soft chew sounds, a beat apart.
- 4.0–5.5s: Music bed lifts — same Latina-coded warm bed as Spot #1 (Suno v4, can reuse the composition for sonic continuity across the campaign).
- 5.5–6.0s: Beat resolves clean.

**VO:** Valentina — **"Mi rutina."** (2 words, ES, delivered 0.0–1.0s as a soft voiceover.) Ship an EN-VO variant ("My routine.") and an ES-VO variant in parallel — let matched-language ad serving pick. Caption pair on screen for the no-VO fallback.

**On-screen text:**

- 0.0–1.0s: `LA RUTINA` (or `THE ROUTINE` on the EN variant) — lower-third, off-white, 60% opacity.
- 5.5–6.0s: End card — both product names visible, single bundle URL, split accent bar.

**Production note:** No founder shoot needed. Bottle-on-counter beats (0–1s, 4–5.5s tail wide) shoot in the same product laydown half-morning as the Trick Daddy laydown if same studio — combine for cost. Founder beats at 1.0–4.0s pulled from Valentina's public IG/TikTok feed by the video guy directly — no text-back needed. End card with split-color accent bar is the only new design element; lock it once and reuse on any future bundle creative. **AOV upside:** this spot drives traffic to the home page (both products visible). If/when SinFiltros builds a bundle SKU on Shopify, swap the URL to the bundle PDP and watch AOV jump.

## Production & distribution notes

- **Total Valentina time ask:** under 10 minutes.
  - Consent text/SMS thread (5 min)
  - Optional 60-second phone VO recording (or skip and use ElevenLabs v3 clone with signed consent — train on her public TikTok audio).
- **No "send me clips" ask.** Video guy pulls all archival founder beats directly from her public IG (`@valentinasinfiltros`, 6.5M+) and TikTok feed. He picks the strongest direct-to-camera frames per spot — editor's call.
- **Zero shoot day for Valentina.** All three spots build from archive + AI b-roll + lifestyle inserts + a half-morning product laydown for bottle/counter beats.
- **Consent:** SMS thread acceptable (close relationship). Save to `assets/consent/sinfiltros/`. Specific scope: SinFiltros product ads, VURT distribution, public-feed clip reuse, ElevenLabs voice clone if used.
- **No FTC-flagged claims.** No "proven," no "clinical," no "boosts." Name the problem (PMS / skin · hair · nails / morning routine) and the product. That's the ad.
- **End cards** lock to a fixed grid: cream background, near-black wordmark, accent color in product name + tagline only. Moodmies = pink accent. Glowmies = powder-blue accent. Spot #3 (stack) = split accent bar with both colors. Same typography across all three.
- **Bilingual rotation (distribution layer, not script layer):** for each of the three spots, ship an EN-caption + EN-VO variant and an ES-caption + ES-VO variant. Run them against matched-language audience segments. Read CTR at 72h. Budget shifts toward winning language from Day 4. ES often outperforms EN against Latina-targeted segments — `cólicos` reads sharper than `dolores`, `la rutina` reads sharper than `the routine`. That's six creative deliverables total (3 spots × 2 languages), not three.
- **No promo codes wave 1.** When codes exist, build a 4th spot (price-stack wedge) for Wave 2.
- **Ingredient mentions:** zero in any spot. Save them for the PDP — they tank the 6s convert beat.
