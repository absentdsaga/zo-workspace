## Production notes
- Trick's voice is the product. Do not dub, do not polish into generic "food content" narration. Leave him as he speaks.
- If Trick shoots cooking content already (TikTok / IG archive), we can license a pull of his existing footage as b-roll for the 15s cuts — saves a whole shoot day.
- Sunday's Eatery sequences need location permission — confirm separately.
- Food styling should be real food the audience recognizes. Not fancy restaurant plating. Regular-kitchen-level plating.
- VURT cross-promo: on shows where Trick appears, prioritize this creative in-rotation (viewers already recognize him — CTR lift expected).
