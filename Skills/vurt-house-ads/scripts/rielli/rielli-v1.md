# RIELLI — Ad Scripts v1

Brand voice: editorial, quiet, confident. No hype words. Music-forward. Vertical 9:16, audio-on.

Spoken-word budget: ~30 words for 15s, ~65 words for 30s (leaves breathing room).

CTA pattern: `riellibrand.com · WELCOME10`

---

## 15s #1 — Luxury / Identity — "Not One Of Many"

**Visual beats:**
- 0.0–1.5s: MACRO SHOT — a single Rielli swimsuit tag being stitched by hand, threading the model's initial or a number "01"
- 1.5–6s: SLOW PAN across three women on a beach, each in a *different* Rielli piece (proof of one-of-a-kind)
- 6–12s: Cut to a mirror shot — one woman alone, fastening her top, confident gaze, not smiling
- 12–15s: Product laydown + brand wordmark + WELCOME10

**VO (14 words):**
"You're not one of many. Your swimwear shouldn't be either. Rielli. Made one piece at a time."

**On-screen copy (end card):**
`RIELLI · riellibrand.com · WELCOME10 for 10% off`

**Sound:** Ambient resort synth, soft wave texture under. No drop.

---

## 15s #2 — Craftsmanship — "Miami Atelier"

**Visual beats:**
- 0.0–1.5s: HANDS ONLY — fabric shears cutting printed silk, clean snip, sound foregrounded
- 1.5–7s: BTS at Miami atelier — needle through fabric, tag being clipped, fabric folded
- 7–12s: Cut to the finished piece on a hanger, then on a body at golden hour
- 12–15s: End card

**VO (11 words):** (can be silent-with-text if preferred)
"Not mass produced. Made in Miami. Made for you."

**On-screen text cues:**
- 1.5s: "MIAMI ATELIER"
- 7s: "ONE OF ONE"
- 12s: `RIELLI · riellibrand.com`

**Sound:** Diegetic atelier sounds (scissors, sewing machine) carry the first 7 seconds. Soft piano under the reveal.

## 15s #3 — Travel — "Worn Wherever You Go"

**Visual beats:**
- 0.0–1.5s: Drone pull-back over turquoise water (Bali or Bahamas type — stock or AI-gen ok)
- 1.5–8s: Woman walking the shoreline, suit movement clearly visible, editorial wardrobe
- 8–13s: Jump cut through 3 locations — pool deck, boat bow, sand — same woman, same suit reads true
- 13–15s: End card

**VO (13 words):**
"Worn in Bali. Designed in Miami. Made for wherever you go next."

**On-screen text:**
- 1.5s: "BALI"
- 5s: "BAHAMAS"
- 8s: "MIAMI"

**Sound:** Warm guitar or muted synth travel cue.

---

## 15s #4 — Credibility — "Globally Worn"

**Visual beats:**
- 0.0–1.5s: Fast cuts of editorial covers / press clippings / retail placements (kept tasteful — no logo borrowing)
- 1.5–8s: Steady shot on a Rielli piece on a model at a private resort
- 8–13s: Retail placement — Rielli rack at a destination store
- 13–15s: End card

**VO (14 words):**
"Forbes recognized. Globally worn. Handmade in Miami. Rielli — luxury, made one piece at a time."

**Note:** Use only credibility items Rielli has provided publicly on their press page. Do not name-check Kardashians in a 15s — save for the 30s where context protects it.

## 30s #1 — Founder — "Built Without Permission"

**Visual beats:**
- 0.0–2s: Arielle at her atelier, back to camera, hand over a piece of fabric
- 2–8s: She turns — lets silence sit — begins
- 8–22s: Cuts of atelier → woman on a beach → Arielle returning to the bench
- 22–27s: Arielle on camera, close
- 27–30s: End card

**VO — Arielle (60 words):**
"I started Rielli because I couldn't find what I wanted to wear. So I made it. One piece. Then another. No investors. No shortcuts. Just a needle, a pattern, and a belief that swimwear should feel like you — not like everyone else. Today Rielli is worn from Miami to Bali. Still made by hand. Still one piece at a time."

**End card:** `RIELLI · riellibrand.com · WELCOME10`

---

## 30s #2 — Craft Narrative — "One Piece At A Time"

**Visual beats:**
- 0.0–3s: Pattern paper laid out, scissors hovering
- 3–10s: Cuts through atelier work — stitching, trimming, tagging
- 10–18s: Piece moves to a body — woman at resort, different light
- 18–25s: Intercut a second piece — *noticeably different print* — on a second woman in a different location
- 25–30s: End card

**VO (55 words):**
"Every Rielli piece begins with one cut. One pattern. One woman in mind. Because no two women should wear the same swimsuit the same way. From our Miami atelier to your next destination. Rielli. Luxury, made one piece at a time."

**End card:** `RIELLI · riellibrand.com · WELCOME10`

---

## Notes for production
- All end cards follow the same grid — wordmark top, URL middle, offer bottom.
- Never cut a 30s into a 15s automatically — the 15s here are designed from zero, not trims.
- If Arielle isn't available for 30s #1, flip to a VO-only edit with an actress's hands only and keep the narration in first person (less powerful — push for real founder).
