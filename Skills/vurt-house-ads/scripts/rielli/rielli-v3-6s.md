# RIELLI — Ad Scripts (6-second spec)

**Priority brand per Ted Lucas.** Spec: **6s, 1080x1920 vertical, audio-on, non-skippable, clickable end card via VAST.**

## What wins at 6s in luxury swim DTC (Skims Swim, Hunza G, Oseree benchmark)

The category-winning beat structure:
- **0–1s:** Water motion (drip, splash, fabric hitting water) — pattern interrupt, no logo yet.
- **1–3s:** Body in piece, in motion (walk into ocean, turn, dive). Founder body if available, anonymous body if not. Back-turn always — face-forward at 6s reads as model casting, not founder.
- **3–5s:** Texture money shot — macro on fabric, hardware, tie, seam. **This is the conversion frame.** It answers "what is this fabric actually like" — the question that gets a viewer to click.
- **5–6s:** Locked end card. Wordmark + URL + one differentiator line.

**Audio:** ambient water + soft synth bed. **No VO.** Category does not win on narration.

**The mistake to avoid:** Slow zooms on stills with no cut between body and macro. Treat each 6s like a 3-shot cut, not a slideshow.

## What's unique to Rielli (the wedge)

Most luxury swim is "Italy by team." Rielli is **made-in-Miami by Arielle Rielli, by hand.** Every spot has to make this legible without a single voiced word. Atelier hands + Miami light + her body (silhouette) is the proof stack.

## Palette + end card

Rielli does not publish a brand color system. Site is Shopify generic black/white. End card pulls from actual product photography:
- Background: cream / off-white pulled from atelier daylight frames
- Wordmark: deep brown / near-black `#20130D` (dominant photography tone)
- Accent line: warm beige `#BCB6AE` (also from product photography)
- Type: serif wordmark, sans for URL line

**Pull exact hexes from the hero product still used in each spot — do not lock to invented brand colors.**

End-card content options (pick one per spot):
- `RIELLI · MADE IN MIAMI · riellibrand.com`
- `RIELLI · BY HAND · riellibrand.com`
- `RIELLI · riellibrand.com` (neutral, lowest-friction)

## Spot #1 — "Worn" (water hook → body → texture → end card)

**Wedge:** Hero conversion spot. Hits the category-winning pattern exactly: water at 0s, body at 1s, texture macro at 3s, end card at 5s. Run as the always-on workhorse.

**Visual beats (6.0s total):**
- **0.0–1.0s:** ECU water hits fabric. Single drop slow-mo onto the swim piece, fabric darkens where wet, light fractures through. Phone-shot OR Veo 3.1 image-to-video from a real product still.
- **1.0–3.0s:** Cut to **Arielle from behind**, walking into Miami ocean at golden hour, in the piece. Slow walk. Phone-shot. If atelier/beach access blocked: AI anonymous-body silhouette in Veo 3.1, golden-hour ocean reference.
- **3.0–5.0s:** **Texture money shot.** ECU on the piece — seam, tie, hardware, or rib texture. This is the hold beat. 50mm macro feel, slight motion (water still dripping, fabric still settling).
- **5.0–6.0s:** End card. `RIELLI` wordmark, `MADE IN MIAMI · riellibrand.com`. Click target = full frame.

**Audio:**
- 0.0–1.0s: Single water drop, then drip-tail.
- 1.0–3.0s: Ocean ambient lift, distant.
- 3.0–5.0s: Soft synth bed enters, one warm chord.
- 5.0–6.0s: Chord resolves. Clean end.

**VO:** None.

**On-screen text:** End card only.

**Production note:** 100% AI-generatable if atelier/beach blocked. Feed real product still as image-to-video reference. Stitch 3 short AI clips (1s + 2s + 2s) — never generate a single 6s clip, physics drifts.

## Spot #2 — "By Hand" (proof wedge — atelier → garment → end card)

**Wedge:** Answers "why isn't this Oseree at half the price." The hand-stitching frame is the entire luxury argument. Run this against retargeting audiences who've visited the site once.

**Visual beats (6.0s total):**
- **0.0–1.5s:** ECU **needle through fabric.** Real hands (Arielle's or atelier team's), natural window light. Thread pulls taut. Phone macro or Veo macro from a stitch reference.
- **1.5–3.5s:** **Cut wider** — full piece on dress form, slow rotation in window light. Daylight, off-white wall behind. The whole garment readable in one shot.
- **3.5–5.0s:** **Texture money shot** — ECU on the finished tag or seam, brand mark visible (`RIELLI` printed on label).
- **5.0–6.0s:** End card. `RIELLI · BY HAND · MADE IN MIAMI · riellibrand.com`.

**Audio:**
- 0.0–1.5s: Thread pull, fabric tension sound, ambient room tone.
- 1.5–3.5s: One held minor-key warm note.
- 3.5–5.0s: Note holds.
- 5.0–6.0s: Resolves. Clean end.

**VO:** None.

**On-screen text:** End card only. Optional subtle subtitle line above URL: `One at a time.`

**Production note:** If atelier access blocked, AI-generate the needle ECU from a tag still; phone-shoot the dress-form rotation in any window-lit room (white wall + dress form, 20 minutes). Real hands beat AI hands every time at 6s — push for one phone clip of stitching even if rest is AI.

## Spot #3 — "Out" (lifestyle conversion wedge — golden-hour body → texture → end card)

**Wedge:** Aspirational close. The "I want to be in this piece on this beach" frame. Run on prospecting audiences as the third A/B against Spots #1 and #2 — different hook, same end card so brand recall compounds.

**Visual beats (6.0s total):**
- **0.0–1.0s:** **Light hits the piece** — slow pan across fabric on a body, golden-hour sun across skin and seam. No face yet, just body + sun + fabric. Phone or AI from product still.
- **1.0–3.0s:** Pull to mid — Arielle in the piece, **back-turn**, on a Miami balcony or stepping toward the water. Slow movement. Phone-shot at her atelier window or AI anonymous body in golden-hour scene.
- **3.0–5.0s:** **Texture money shot** — ECU on the silhouette of the piece against backlit sun (rim light through the fabric). The rim-light beat is the visual signature — make it readable.
- **5.0–6.0s:** End card. `RIELLI · riellibrand.com`. (Cleanest of the three end cards — let the spot end on quiet brand.)

**Audio:**
- 0.0–1.0s: Soft wind, one breath of distant ocean.
- 1.0–3.0s: Ambient ocean lift continues.
- 3.0–5.0s: Single warm pad enters.
- 5.0–6.0s: Pad resolves.

**VO:** None.

**On-screen text:** End card only.

**Production note:** If beach access blocked, AI-generate the entire spot — golden-hour Miami balcony with anonymous body in product. Push for one real phone-shot of Arielle in piece for at least one of the three Rielli spots — distribution carries the founder signal across the rotation.

## Production & distribution notes

- **Arielle shoot ask if available:** one 60–90 min phone shoot at atelier (window light) and/or beach (golden hour). Phone only (iPhone 15 Pro+, 4K60). Never on-camera speaking. Silhouette / back-turn / hand work. Covers all three spots.
- **If atelier + beach both blocked:** all three spots are 100% AI-generatable from real product stills. Use Veo 3.1 (primary) or Kling 2.5 (fallback) image-to-video. Higgsfield for any complex camera move.
- **Stitch don't generate.** Every spot is 2–4 short AI clips stitched, never one long generation. Physics drifts past 2s.
- **Finish pass:** AI clips → Magnific Video. Live clips → Topaz. Never ship raw AI — finishing is what kills the "generated" tell.
- **Texture money shot is non-negotiable.** Spot is built around the 3.0–5.0s macro frame. If no macro, the spot is a slideshow, not an ad. Shoot or generate texture before anything else.
- **End card** is fixed across all three spots: cream/off-white background with wordmark in deep brown, URL below, optional differentiator line. Same typography, same composition. Click target = full frame, not URL text only.
- **No offer, no %, no urgency, no founder VO.** Luxury swim category does not respond to it.
- **VURT distribution:** all three live in rotation from launch. 6s frequency cap is high — same audience can see each spot 5x without fatigue. Read CTR + completion at 72h, kill the bottom of the three, ship a fresh hook into Wave 2.
