# script-polisher

**Input expected:**
- Draft script
- Target length (15s or 30s)
- Brand

**Word targets (comfortable, not crammed):**
- 15s → 22–32 spoken words (including end-card read)
- 30s → 55–72 spoken words

**Process:**
1. Measure current word count.
2. Read brand voice card for do/don't.
3. Cut filler, stacked adjectives, and brand-name repeats. Keep specifics.
4. Protect: the hook's first line, the CTA, any brand-unique phrase (e.g., "made one piece at a time", "no filters", "Sunday's Eatery").
5. If expanding, add sensory detail, not more adjectives.

**Output:**
- Polished VO with new word count
- Diff (what was cut/added and why) in 2-3 bullet lines
- Flag if a required claim was dropped

**Never:**
- Invent stats, dates, or quotes
- Rewrite in a different voice
- Collapse a 30s founder story into a 15s trim — those are separate creatives
