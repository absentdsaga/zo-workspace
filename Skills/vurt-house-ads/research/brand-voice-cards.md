# Brand Voice Cards — VURT House Ads First Wave

## 1. RIELLI

**What it is:** Luxury resortwear / swimwear. Handmade in Miami atelier. One-of-a-kind pieces. Price: $98–$300. Promo: WELCOME10 (10% off first order). Free tote above $250.

**Founder:** Arielle C. Baril. Forbes-recognized. Worn by Kim & Kourtney Kardashian. Sold in Bali and the Bahamas.

**Socials:**
- IG: @rielli (primary) — editorial imagery, resort shoots, atelier BTS
- TikTok: @rielli — slower adoption, founder POV
- Site: riellibrand.com

**Core tension:** Mass swimwear vs. made-for-you luxury. Bali/Bahamas aspiration vs. Miami craftsmanship.

**Target viewer on VURT:** Women 25–44, urban/Latina lean, drama-watchers, aspirational lifestyle, plan vacations 2+/year, buy premium fashion online.

**Voice:**
- Elevated, quiet, confident
- Editorial, not salesy
- Minimal copy, let craftsmanship and body language carry
- No exclamation marks. No hype words.
- Think Chloé/Aerie crossed with Jacquemus restraint

**Do:**
- Show hands cutting/sewing. Show an atelier detail shot.
- Let silence sit. Music bed, not VO-heavy.
- Reference real places (Bali, Bahamas, Miami).
- Name the Kardashian mention only in founder/credibility cuts — don't lean on it in every ad.

**Don't:**
- Stack "stunning, gorgeous, luxurious" adjectives
- Use emojis in on-screen copy
- Flash prices (premium logic — discount is subtext via WELCOME10)
- Show try-on hauls or influencer-unbox energy

**Angles (from brief):**
1. Luxury / Identity
2. Craftsmanship / Slow fashion
3. Aspirational / Travel
4. Founder / Credibility

## 2. SINFILTROS NUTRITION

**What it is:** Women's wellness supplements. Two SKUs today:
- **MOODMIES** — PMS support gummies — $26.99
- **GLOWMIES** — Hair, skin & nails gummies — $26.99
- **Bundle** — $49.99

**Founder:** Valentina Castañeda (sinfiltrosvale). Building the brand in public via her own channels. Large Latina female following.

**Socials:**
- IG: @sinfiltrosnutrition
- TikTok: @sinfiltrosnutrition + founder's personal @sinfiltrosvale
- Founder TikTok is Spanish-dominant with 6M+ following (verify in research/)

**Target viewer on VURT:** Latinas 22–40, tired of fad diets, cycle-aware, beauty-from-within mindset, follows wellness creators, responds to Spanish-first content.

**Voice:**
- Honest, clean, relatable
- Slightly aspirational — never "magical transformation"
- Founder-forward — Valentina's voice is the brand
- Bilingual-native: Spanish primary, English secondary
- No filters (literal name of brand) — no doctored promises

**Do:**
- Cycle-reality hooks ("day 3 of my period")
- Plain-language benefits (mood, bloating, hair growth, skin)
- Before/after must be honest (4-6 week reality, not 3 days)
- Spanish cuts should lead — these convert highest for this audience

**Don't:**
- Promise "instant" anything
- Use clinical/medical voice (that's a different brand)
- Over-index on beauty — PMS angle is differentiator
- Skip disclaimer where required

**Angles (from brief):**
1. PMS angle ("your cycle shouldn't control your month")
2. Beauty-from-within angle
3. Bundle / combined

## 3. TRICK DADDY POTS

**What it is:** 15-piece ceramic cookware collection. Ceramic non-stick, heat-resistant, dishwasher-friendly. $169.99. Full set: fry pans, saucepans, stock pots, tempered glass lids, multi-use utensils.

**Founder:** Trick Daddy (Miami rap icon, owner of Sunday's Eatery). **IMPORTANT: Trick Daddy is already on VURT's talent roster** — we have direct talent access.

**Socials:**
- IG: @trickdaddypots (brand) + @trickdaddydollars (Trick's personal)
- TikTok: @trickdaddydollars — huge organic cooking content library
- Site: trickdaddypots.com

**Core tension:** Not "display cookware" — real tools for real kitchens where people cook, host, feed family.

**Target viewer on VURT:** Women 28–55, family cooks, Black/urban lean, Miami/Southern culture affinity, already watch his content or his culture.

**Voice:**
- Warm, cultural, authentic
- Confident but not flashy
- Community-driven — "food brings people together"
- Trick's voice can carry the whole ad — he's a great talker
- Use AAVE naturally where Trick would say it — don't sanitize

**Do:**
- Use Trick on-camera or VO (we have talent access)
- Show real food: seafood boil, oxtail, Sunday dinner
- Keep price visible ($169.99 for 15-piece is the value story)
- Lean into Sunday's Eatery lineage
- Family/hands-across-table visuals

**Don't:**
- Make it a celebrity-endorsement ad ("I partnered with…")
- Sanitize the voice into generic cooking-show speak
- Hide the fact that Trick cooks — his content already proves it
- Treat it as a gag — it's a real product

**Angles (from brief):**
1. Culture / Family
2. Pride / Identity
3. Creator-led authenticity
4. Product / Value

## Cross-brand: VURT placement context

These run *inside* VURT's vertical micro-series. Viewer is mid-story, audio-on, attention is high but fragile — first 1.5s decides completion. All three voices should feel like they *belong next to the content*, not like they fell in from a Facebook feed.
