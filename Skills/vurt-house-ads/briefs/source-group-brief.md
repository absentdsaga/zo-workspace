## ***RIELLI***

**Rielli** is a luxury resortwear brand redefining swimwear through individuality, craftsmanship, and global appeal.

Founded by **Arielle C. Baril**, the brand has grown from a passion project into a globally recognized label, worn by icons like Kim and Kourtney Kardashian and sold in destinations like Bali and the Bahamas — all without traditional backing.

At its core, Rielli is about:

* **Confidence through design**  
* **Individuality over mass production**  
* **Quality over quantity**  
* **Global luxury with intentional roots**

Each piece is designed, cut, sewn, and packaged in a Miami atelier, reinforcing a commitment to **ethical, slow fashion**and elevated craftsmanship.

**Product Brief**

Rielli offers **luxury swimwear and resortwear**, designed to make women feel confident, comfortable, and distinct.

Unlike mass-produced swimwear brands, Rielli creates pieces **one by one**, ensuring that every item reflects the individuality of the person wearing it.

**Key Features:**

* Handmade in Miami  
* Limited, one-of-a-kind production  
* Premium materials and construction  
* Designed for both aesthetic and comfort

**Positioning**

This is not fast fashion.

Rielli is **intentional luxury** — where every piece is made to reflect the uniqueness of the woman wearing it.
