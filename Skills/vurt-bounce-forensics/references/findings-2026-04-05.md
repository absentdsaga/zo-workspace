# VURT Paid Ads Bounce Forensics

**Date:** April 5, 2026
**Analysis period:** March 29 - April 4, 2026
**Total paid sessions analyzed:** 10,566
**Overall paid bounce rate:** 82.1%

---

## THE #1 REASON: Users Land on a Black Screen + Age Gate

When someone clicks a VURT ad on Facebook or Instagram, here is exactly what they experience:

1. They tap the ad in their feed
2. Facebook/Instagram's in-app browser opens
3. They see a **completely black video player** (no thumbnail, no trailer, no content visible)
4. An **age gate modal** immediately covers the screen: "ARE YOU 17 YEARS OR OLDER?"
5. There is zero visual payoff. No preview of the show. No hook. Just darkness and a legal prompt.
6. They leave.

**The data proves this:**

| Metric | Value | What it means |
|--------|-------|--------------|
| Paid sessions | 10,566 | Total ad clicks in 7 days |
| Video starts | **5** | Only 5 out of 10,566 sessions started a video (0.05%) |
| User engagement (10s+) | 646 | Only 6% stayed longer than 10 seconds |
| Screen rendered | ~2,919 | Only 28% had Angular finish rendering |
| Events per session | 3.3 | Most sessions fire exactly: page_view + session_start + first_visit, then exit |

**93% of paid traffic lands on series detail pages** (not the homepage). The homepage bounces at 65.6%. Series pages bounce at 75-100%. The difference: series pages show the black player + age gate immediately.

## SECONDARY FACTORS (significant but not #1)

### A. 63% of Paid Traffic is Outside the US

| Country | Sessions | Bounce | Issue |
|---------|----------|--------|-------|
| United States | 3,831 | 80.8% | Target market, still bouncing |
| Trinidad & Tobago | 1,939 | 77.9% | Why are we paying for this? |
| Tanzania | 1,236 | 93.8% | Pure waste |
| Nigeria | 1,148 | 81.6% | Not target market |
| Kenya | 816 | 84.2% | Not target market |

VURT is a US platform. 6,735 of 10,566 paid sessions (63%) are from non-US countries. This is an ad targeting problem the paid ads team (SimpleSocial/Alex) needs to fix immediately.

### B. One Campaign is Dramatically Worse

| Campaign | Platform | Sessions | Bounce |
|----------|----------|----------|--------|
| 120245604984150029 | Facebook | 3,468 | **92.0%** |
| 120247130906080029 | Instagram | 1,930 | 69.9% |
| 120247130906080029 | Facebook | 1,478 | 69.8% |
| VURT $2500 Digital Ads | Instagram | 1,704 | 80.7% |

Campaign `120245604984150029` is the worst performer at 92% bounce across 3,468 sessions. The same amount spent on campaign `120247130906080029` would get 22 percentage points better performance.

### C. 5MB JavaScript Payload

The Angular SPA ships ~5MB of JavaScript. On mobile Facebook/Instagram in-app browsers, this takes 5-10+ seconds to download and execute. 72% of users leave before Angular finishes hydrating -- they never even see the interactive page.

### D. CloudFront Caching HTML for 14+ Hours

The homepage HTML has been cached by CloudFront for 52,348 seconds (14.5 hours). No Cache-Control header is set. If content or meta tags change, users see stale HTML until the cache expires.

### E. April 4 Anomaly -- 99.9% Bounce

| Date | Sessions | Bounce |
|------|----------|--------|
| Mar 29 | 3,889 | 91.4% |
| Mar 30 | 413 | 93.5% |
| Mar 31 | 8 | 100% |
| Apr 1 | 1,893 | 71.5% |
| Apr 2 | 2,080 | 71.3% |
| Apr 3 | 1,509 | 74.2% |

Something broke again on April 4. The bounce went from 74% back to 99.9%.

## WHAT ACTUALLY FIXES THIS

**In order of impact:**

### 1. Fix the landing page experience (THE FIX)
The ad should deep-link to a page where **video content is immediately visible and playing**. Right now:
- Video player is BLACK (no thumbnail, no autoplay working)
- Age gate blocks everything
- No visual hook exists

**Options:**
- A. Show a show poster/thumbnail in the player area BEFORE the age gate
- B. Autoplay a trailer behind the age gate (semi-transparent overlay)
- C. Build dedicated landing pages for ad campaigns with hero video + clear CTA
- D. At minimum: show the show's poster image instead of a black rectangle

### 2. Fix ad targeting -- US only
Tell Alex/SimpleSocial: restrict geo-targeting to US (and maybe Canada/UK). 63% of budget is going to Tanzania, Nigeria, Kenya, Trinidad & Tobago.

### 3. Pause campaign 120245604984150029
This one FB campaign has 3,468 sessions at 92% bounce. That's 22 points worse than other campaigns. Either fix the creative/targeting or pause it.

### 4. Reduce JavaScript payload
5MB JS on mobile in-app browsers = most users never see the interactive page. This is Dev Team Ops List item #9 (SSR).

### 5. Fix CloudFront HTML caching
Set `Cache-Control: no-cache, no-store` on HTML responses so users always get fresh content.
