# Bound — Caption Guide
## Scene: Walter's Office (The Reprimand)

**Show:** Bound — VURT Original (psychological thriller)
**Status:** COMING SOON pre-launch tease
**CTA:** "Stream when it drops on VURT"
**Slot:** 70 (originals)
**Arc placement:** Mid-film tension/setup beat. Pairs with the role-flip climax as the "before/after" — this is the lowest-status moment that makes the takedown hit.

**Scene:** Michelle (Charisma Carpenter) getting reamed in her boss Walter's (Daniel Baldwin) office. Workplace shame beat — she was seen in public with Ryan (the "drug-dealing car thief" who, audience knows, is her abuser) and had sex with someone in the office bathroom. This is her spiral made public — Walter's disappointment is the mirror that shows how far gone she is.

**Hook line locked:** *"I'm so sick and tired of hearing those words come out of your lips."*

**Brand guardrails:**
- Don't spoil Ryan as abuser
- Don't lead with "had sex in the bathroom" (reframes as scandal, not psychological breakdown)
- Lead with shame + spiral energy
- No curse words

**Cover frame rule:** Michelle's reaction — eyes down, holding it together — NOT Walter mid-shout. We want viewers feeling her, not him.
