## Search Intent Mapping

### Informational ("I want to learn")
- "what is micro drama" → Blog: "Micro-Drama Explained: The $14B Format You're Not Watching Yet"
- "best short drama apps" → Listicle featuring VURT
- "vertical video streaming explained" → Blog: "Why Vertical Cinema Is the Future of Mobile Storytelling"

### Navigational ("I want to find VURT")
- "VURT app" → App Store / Google Play optimized listing
- "myvurt.com" → Homepage with perfect meta tags

### Transactional ("I want to watch/download")
- "free drama app download" → App Store optimized with these keywords
