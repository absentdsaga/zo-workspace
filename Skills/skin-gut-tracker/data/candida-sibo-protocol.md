# Candida / SIBO / Dysbiosis Protocol — Day-by-Day

Layered on top of the low-nickel + low-histamine diet already in play.

---

## Week 1–2 (Apr 17 – May 1) — STARVE PHASE

**Goal:** Cut fuel to candida + SIBO without killing anything yet. Give the gut lining a calmer environment so L-glutamine can do its job.

### Diet rules (on top of low-nickel)
- NO added sugar. None. Not honey, maple, agave, "just a little."
- NO alcohol.
- NO fruit juice. Whole fruit OK (2–3 servings/day max, low-sugar: berries, green apple).
- NO dairy except butter + aged hard cheese (parmesan, aged cheddar).
- NO bread/pasta/pastry (temporary — reintroduce week 5+).
- Coffee: 1 cup/day max, before noon, black or with butter.

### Supplement schedule
| Time | Supplement | Dose |
|---|---|---|
| Wake | L-Glutamine | 5g in water, empty stomach |
| Breakfast | Zinc picolinate | 30mg with food |
| Breakfast | Vitamin D3 + K2 | 5000IU with fat |
| Breakfast | B-Complex | 1 cap |
| Before lunch | DAO (if histamine meal) | 1 cap, 15 min before |
| Lunch | Quercetin | 500mg |
| Before dinner | DAO (if histamine meal) | 1 cap, 15 min before |
| Dinner | Quercetin | 500mg |
| Bedtime | L-Glutamine | 5g in water |
| Bedtime | Magnesium glycinate | 400mg |

**Keep:** NAC 600mg 2x/day with meals.
**Pause:** Creatine (one variable out).

---

## Week 3–4 (May 1 – May 15) — KILL PHASE 1

**Goal:** Start actively killing candida + reducing bacterial overgrowth. Expect a "die-off" reaction days 1–4 of week 3 (headaches worsen, fatigue, mild flu feeling, possible skin flare). This is normal and means it's working. **Hydrate extra. Don't stop — push through.**

### Add to the stack
| Time | Supplement | Dose |
|---|---|---|
| Breakfast | Berberine 500mg | 1 cap with food |
| Breakfast | S. boulardii | 1 cap with food |
| Dinner | Berberine 500mg | 1 cap with food |

**Still avoiding sugar, alcohol, bread.**

If die-off is intolerable: halve berberine to 1 cap/day for 3 days, then ramp back up.

---

## Week 5–6 (May 15 – May 29) — KILL PHASE 2 + REBUILD

**Goal:** Rotate in a second antimicrobial to hit what berberine missed. Begin reintroducing tolerant foods.

### Swap berberine for oregano oil (2 weeks on, then 1 week off)
| Time | Supplement | Dose |
|---|---|---|
| Breakfast | Oregano oil | 1 cap with food |
| Breakfast | S. boulardii | 1 cap with food |
| Dinner | Oregano oil | 1 cap with food |

### Diet reintro (one food every 3 days, log reaction)
- Week 5 day 1: sourdough bread (fermented — easier on gut)
- Week 5 day 4: small serving white rice
- Week 5 day 7: plain kefir (1/4 cup)
- Week 6: add back low-nickel fruits you miss

---

## Week 7+ — MAINTENANCE

- Drop berberine/oregano.
- Keep: zinc, D3+K2, B-complex, magnesium, quercetin (drop to 1x/day), S. boulardii (drop to 3x/week), L-glutamine (drop to 1x/day morning).
- DAO only before known histamine meals.
- Reintroduce one nickel food every 4 days to find personal threshold.

---

## Red flags — stop and call a doctor
- Severe diarrhea with blood
- Fever >101°F sustained
- Vomiting that won't stop
- Skin flare that's hot, swollen, oozing (could be infected)
- Heart palpitations that don't resolve in an hour
