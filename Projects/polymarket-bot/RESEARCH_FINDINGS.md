# Polymarket Longshot Strategy - Research Findings

## 🔴 CRITICAL FINDINGS - The "Buy Cheap and Pray" Strategy LOSES MONEY

### From 90,000 Wallet Analysis (PANews):

**The Lottery Trap:**
- Strategies focused on longshots (odds < 0.2 / < 20¢) have **NEGATIVE long-term expected value**
- Longshots suffer from **overestimation bias** - they're priced HIGHER than they should be
- "Certainty trap" (odds > 0.8) also loses due to poor risk/reward

**The Golden Range:**
- **0.2-0.4 odds (20-40¢)** is the "Golden Odds" range
- This range has exploitable market mispricing
- Best balance of risk/reward

**Favorite-Longshot Bias (AGAINST Longshots):**
- Traders consistently OVERVALUE longshots
- From Kalshi analysis: **5¢ contracts won only 4% of the time** (should win 5%)
- This is a NEGATIVE edge for longshot buyers

### From Top Trader Analysis (27,000 trades):

**Winners DON'T Use Simple Longshot Buying:**
- Top traders use **complex hedging** and **probability transformation**
- They manage **risk/reward ratios**, not win rates
- Successful strategies involve position sizing and quick profit-taking

**Real Win Rates:**
- Top traders: 50-55% win rate (not 77%+ as it appears)
- High win rates are illusions from unclosed losing positions

### From 72M Kalshi Trades:

**"Optimism Tax":**
- Impulsive buyers ("Takers") favor longshot bets
- Market Makers profit from this predictable behavior
- Retail traders accepting 9:1 risk/reward lose systematically
