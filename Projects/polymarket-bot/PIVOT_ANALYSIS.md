# Strategy Pivot Analysis

## What We Learned (The Hard Way)

### ❌ What DOESN'T Work:
- **5-minute market arbitrage** - 10% fees kill any edge
- **Sum-to-one arbitrage** - Markets too efficient, bots already farming
- **Low-latency plays** - Need co-location, not possible from here

### ✅ What COULD Work:

## Option 1: Directional Trading (Momentum/TA Based)

**Strategy:** Bet on BTC direction in 5-min markets using technical signals

**Pros:**
- Can use 5-min markets (high frequency opportunities)
- No need for tight spreads
- Maker fee rebates if using limit orders

**Cons:**
- Not arbitrage (you can lose)
- Requires TA/momentum signals
- 50/50 base probability

**Viability:** Medium - Need edge from signals

---

## Option 2: Traditional Event Markets

**Strategy:** Find mispriced events on main Polymarket

**Examples:**
- Political events
- Sports outcomes  
- Economic indicators
- Entertainment/pop culture

**Pros:**
- Lower fees on some markets
- Longer timeframes = less competition
- Can find genuine mispricings

**Cons:**
- Need research/edge
- Lower frequency
- Tie up capital longer

**Viability:** High - This is what most Polymarket traders do

---

## Option 3: News-Based Trading

**Strategy:** Trade immediately on breaking news

**How:**
- Monitor news feeds (Twitter, Bloomberg, etc.)
- Be first to trade before market adjusts
- Exit when market catches up

**Pros:**
- Clear edge (information asymmetry)
- Works on regular Polymarket markets
- Repeatable strategy

**Cons:**
- Need fast news sources
- Competition from bots
- Requires speed

**Viability:** Medium-High with good setup

---

## Option 4: Statistical Arbitrage

**Strategy:** Find markets with correlated outcomes

**Examples:**
- "Trump wins" vs "Republican wins" - should be correlated
- State-by-state election markets vs national
- Crypto prices across different prediction markets

**Pros:**
- True arbitrage (less risk)
- Longer timeframes
- Less competition

**Cons:**
- Hard to find opportunities
- Need to find correlated markets
- Capital intensive

**Viability:** Medium - Requires research

---
