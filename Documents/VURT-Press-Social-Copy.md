```
Last week, TechCrunch covered our launch.

"Meet Vurt, the mobile-first streaming platform for indie filmmakers embracing vertical video."

The micro-drama market hit $14 billion this year. ReelShort alone is projected at $1.2B in consumer spending. DramaBox: $276M. Even TikTok launched its own micro-drama app in January.

But every single platform in this space has the same problem: no cultural identity, no community, and creators don't own anything.

We built VURT to fix that.

- Free to watch (ad-supported, not paywalled)

The way people consume content has already changed. We're building something that fits that future.

If you're a filmmaker, a creator, or someone who believes indie storytelling deserves a real platform -- we'd love to hear from you.

Download VURT on the App Store or Google Play, or visit myvurt.com.
```
