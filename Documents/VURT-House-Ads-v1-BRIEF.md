First wave of VURT house ads — Source Group portfolio brands running inside VURT's 30s pre-roll slot (non-skippable, audio-on, clickable end-card via VAST). This is a **one-week turnaround**, **zero external-creator spend** campaign designed to drive direct-response traffic to each brand's site.

## Creative direction (what will be different from every other DTC ad in feed)

**Rielli** — No logo-first open, no Miami-Beach-pool cliché. Lead with *fabric in motion* and Arielle's hands at the atelier bench. Her on-camera delivery (~11 sec total across both 30s) is the unreproducible moat — Oseree, Agua, Faithfull can't buy back "real founder, Miami, one at a time." Quiet confidence register. No % off language.

**SinFiltros** — Symptom-first hooks (the verified winning pattern for women's-wellness DTC in 2026). Spanglish code-switch. No millennial pink — terracotta / olive / warm cream palette. Telenovela-style cold open on the 30s matches VURT's drama-environment audience. Valentina's **6.5M TikTok following** (verified live) is our single biggest unfair advantage — her face + voice = social proof for free.

**Trick Daddy Pots** — Zero new footage of Trick. The entire spot is built from his existing cooking-show archive (episodes with Mike Epps, Trina, Ms Pat, Rico Recklezz, Ice Cube, Lil Duval) + product laydowns. The argument: "every cook-up the audience already watched on his channel was in these pots — *that's* the ad." Miami-bass music bed. Shop Pay Installments badge on-screen (never voiced).

## Production approach (what's new / non-obvious)

### Phone-only is the default
Arielle and Valentina will **phone-shoot their own on-camera beats** in their own spaces (apartment, kitchen, bathroom) with window light and a $20 lav mic. Video guy assembles from there. This costs nothing to produce **and** matches the creator-native register VURT's drama audience reads as authentic. A full crew shoot would make the spots feel more "ad" and less "founder" — wrong direction for this platform.

### No paid external creators, no paid models
- Rielli body shots pull from: (a) Arielle's existing SI / Forbes archive she owns, (b) AI-generated bodies in Veo 3.1 with her product photography as image-to-video reference, face never in frame.
- SinFiltros "real women" cuts pull from Valentina's rights-cleared organic UGC (6.5M-follower community) or her team/friends filmed casually.
- Trick spots use zero outside talent.

### ~40% of all footage is AI-generated
Product-in-motion macros, atelier wides, environmental b-roll, telenovela cold opens, anonymous-body lifestyle cuts — all generate-able with Veo 3.1 / Kling 2.5 at 1:1 quality with live footage in 2026. Full AI-vs-live decision framework documented in production brief. Founder-on-camera dialogue, founder VO, and archival talent footage stay real.

### AI stack
Veo 3.1 (primary b-roll) · Runway Gen-4.5 + Act-Two (performance extend) · ElevenLabs v3 (VO cloning) · Suno v4 (music beds) · Magnific Video + Topaz (finishing) · Descript + Premiere Pro (assembly).

## Why this wave is designed to win

1. **Format-accurate.** Non-skippable + audio-on + clickable = this is a captive 30-second TV spot, not a feed ad. Creative built for the slot, not repurposed.
2. **Founder-first where the founder is the moat** (Rielli, SinFiltros). Archive-first where the talent is the moat (Trick).
3. **Matches VURT's content register.** Drama audience + founder register + symptom-naming register + real-kitchen register. No stock "aspirational" energy.
4. **Directly measurable.** VAST event pings + site-side UTMs = we can read VURT → brand conversion brand-by-brand and format-by-format (15s vs 30s) within Wave 1.
5. **Production-cost-lean.** Most of the spend goes to distribution, not production. Perfect fit for a house-ads campaign where the goal is ROAS, not a Cannes reel.
