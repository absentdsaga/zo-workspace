## 6. What to AI vs. what to shoot real (CRITICAL — read before cutting)

This is the decision framework for every shot. Full detail in `Skills/vurt-house-ads/references/ai-video-stack-2026.md` Section 6.

### AI is 1:1 with live in 2026 (generate it — save the shoot day)

Generate with **Veo 3.1** or **Kling 2.5 Turbo**, always feeding a real product still as image-to-video reference when possible:

- **Product in motion** — fabric swirling, pot hitting flame, oil splashing, gummies falling into hand, bottle cap twist, steam rising, liquid pour
- **Macro textures** — thread through fabric, grain in leather, cream into coffee, candle flicker, ceramic fibers
- **Environmental b-roll** — Miami skyline, ocean at golden hour, rain on window, empty countertop morning light, atelier window wide
- **Anonymous-body lifestyle cuts** — woman on a boat (back of head), hand into bowl, feet on tile, body in swimwear turning away, hands folding fabric — **face never forward**
- **Telenovela-style b-roll** — couple mid-argument from behind, walking into bathroom, leaning on door. Wide-to-medium crops of anonymous bodies.
- **"Crowd" and lifestyle inserts** — woman at desk with heating pad, on couch, laughing at dinner table — nobody face-forward or speaking

### AI is close but not there yet — use with caution

- **Face-forward dialogue** — AI can fake a talking face but in non-skippable audio-on contexts the tells accumulate (eye tracking, lip-sync drift, micro-expression smoothness). **Every face-forward dialogue line in this campaign must be real person, real camera.** Runway Act-Two can *extend* a real performance with cloned speech — source performance must be filmed.
- **Hands doing skilled work >3s** — AI drifts on finger count + thread physics. Shoot real.
- **Known-face cameos** — do NOT AI-generate Trick's likeness. Right-of-publicity trap even with consent. Arielle and Valentina are real or Act-Two-extended-from-real only.

### Must shoot real — AI can't fake this yet

- **Founder on-camera dialogue** — Arielle's 18–24s in Rielli 30s; Valentina's 0–3s + 18–25s in SinFiltros 30s #1
- **Founder VO** — Arielle + Valentina voice their own lines. Trick's VO: phone session OR ElevenLabs clone from archive with consent.
- **Archival talent footage** — Trick's cooking show is irreplaceable
- **Phone-shot creator-register** — SinFiltros 15s #4 (the point is it looks organic, AI-faking organic reads fake)

### Per-brand split

| Brand | AI % | Live % | Archive % | IRL shoot cost |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Rielli | ~60 | ~40 | — | 2hr phone shoot |
| SinFiltros | ~50 | ~50 | — | 3hr phone shoot |
| Trick Daddy Pots | ~20 | ~30 | ~50 | Half-day product laydown |

### How to prompt AI video for 1:1 output

1. **Always feed a real product still as image-to-video reference.** Quality jump vs text-only is massive.
2. **Reference grade + lens:** "shot on A7S III, 50mm, natural window light, slight film grain, warm tungsten accent." Not "cinematic" — that triggers the generic-stock look.
3. **Constrain the camera move:** "Static locked-off" or "slow dolly in, 6 inches per second" beats "dynamic camera."
4. **Short clips beat long clips.** Generate in 4–6s beats and cut. Longer clips drift on physics.
5. **Finish with Magnific Video.** AI source → Magnific (adds organic detail). Live source → Topaz. Never skip the finish — untreated AI reads "smooth" / generated.
