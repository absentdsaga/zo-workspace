# VURT House Ads — Consent Templates

**Copy-paste text to send to each talent.** Screenshot their reply and save to `Skills/vurt-house-ads/assets/consent/{trick,rielli,sinfiltros}/`.

Why we need this: ElevenLabs requires a consent attestation to clone a voice. Right-of-publicity law requires documented permission for commercial use of voice and likeness. Informal SMS / iMessage consent is legally sufficient for this scope as long as the thread is saved.

If any of these campaigns scale beyond VURT (paid social beyond VURT, broadcast, extended duration), upgrade to a signed one-pager via DocuSign.
