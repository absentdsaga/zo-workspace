# VURT ABFF Truck — V1: Opening Night
## Friday May 29, 2026 · Gates Hotel South Beach

**Campaign:** VURT video-truck buy, ABFF week (Miami Beach)
**Spot type:** OOH video panels on a moving LED truck — silent, ≤15s

---

## The idea

This is VURT's coming-out moment in Miami. The truck announces **VURT IS HERE**, then frames the rooftop as where the industry meets the platform. The party's real targets are directors, producers, buyers, creators, and press — the people ABFF draws. Consumer walk-ins get the ground floor; the rooftop is the industry room. The QR is the door. Luma is the RSVP mechanism.

**Hero message: "VURT IS HERE."** The party is the answer to what that means, not the subject of the ad.

## Why this works

- **OOH rule: ≤7 words.** "VURT IS HERE" is 3. "Vertical Cinema. Built for the Culture." is 6.
- **Declarative confidence, no CTA.** The brand carries the call. The QR handles the click.
- **B2B framing underneath.** Hosts are cofounders with industry weight (Tarik / Ted / Hilmon). The line "HOSTED BY" signals to a festival audience that this is where the room is.
- **Quotable.** Six words, two beats. Built to be repeated in a dinner conversation, on a panel, or in a Slack thread the morning after.

## SIDE panel (16:9 · 1920×1080 video · SILENT)

Miami Beach §46-152 bans vehicle audio. **15-second spot. Pedestrian + sidewalk audience. All meaning carried by type, motion, cuts.**

| Beat | Time | Visual | Kinetic type |
|------|------|--------|--------------|
| 1 | 0.0–1.5s | Black → VURT logo stamp | Logo holds, one pulse |
| 2 | 1.5–2.5s | Black card | "VURT IS HERE" types in, full hold |
| 3 | 2.5–5.5s | 4–6 rapid frames (0.5–0.6s each) across the VURT slate — talent / show moments, intercut with Miami-night texture. Pulls from whatever's currently live on the myvurt.com homepage rails at ship time | One word per cut, rhythm-locked: **"BUILT" · "FOR" · "THE" · "CULTURE"** |
| 4 | 5.5–8.5s | Title card: "OPENING NIGHT · FRI 5.29" (Core Gold on VURT Black) | Types in, 3-frame hold |
| 5 | 8.5–11.0s | Title card: "ROOFTOP · MIAMI BEACH" + small-caps host line below | Hard cut, hold |
| 6 | 11.0–14.0s | QR pulls center-stage with "SCAN TO REQUEST INVITE" under it | QR zooms in, type anchors below |
| 7 | 14.0–15.0s | VURT logo + `myvurt.com/abff` URL | Logo + URL hold for scan-back |

**Design rules:**

- **No audio.** Silent creative. Do not brief audio.
- **Inter Bold, two weights max.** Skinny fonts vanish on LED.
- **All text legible at 40 ft.**
- **Logo visible in every second.**
- **No ABFF mention.** VURT is not an official sponsor.
- **Talent cuts = brand texture.** Fast-rotation across many faces is fine without individual OOH releases. Consent only triggers when a single face becomes the hero of a frame (looks like sponsorship).

## Stickiness loop (after the scan)

**Landing page (`myvurt.com/abff` before 5/29):**

- Headline: **"VURT is here."**
- Subhead: *"Vertical Cinema. Built for the Culture. Fri 5.29 is our opening night in Miami Beach."*
- Two buttons:
  - **[Request rooftop invite →]** (primary, Core Gold fill) — links out to Luma event page
  - **[Ground floor walk-in info →]** (secondary, outline) — stays on `myvurt.com/abff`

**Rooftop path (Luma-managed):**

1. Button → Luma event page.
2. Guest requests via Luma's standard form (name, email, "how you know VURT" optional field).
3. Luma waitlist + approval — Dioni / team review and approve directly in Luma.
4. Luma handles all reminder emails (approval confirmation, 48h prior, day-of).
5. Post-event 5.30 AM: VURT sends a recap link on its own list (not Luma) pointing back to a recap on `myvurt.com`.

**Ground-floor path (walk-in):**

1. Info page: address + Fri 5.29 doors open time + "open to public, 21+ with ID."
2. Lightweight email capture: "Tell me when the next VURT thing hits Miami."
3. Day-of 5.29 PM email: reminder + "if the rooftop looks full, downstairs is where it's happening."
4. Post-event: add to general VURT newsletter.

Each touch re-routes to VURT web/app. That's the whole point.

## Variants (for A/B inside V1 rotation)

- **"500 cap"** — subline "500 ON THE ROOF · SCAN TO REQUEST" frames scarcity without saying "exclusive."
- **"Quiet"** — near-black panel with only "VURT IS HERE," "FRI 5.29," and a flickering QR. Restraint pattern (A24-style). Stands out against LED spam. Highest aesthetic upside, lowest information density.
- **"Show-first"** — lead with one signature show still instead of "VURT IS HERE." Hero line becomes "OPENING NIGHT · FRI 5.29 · VURT." Works when a specific show is the hook more than the brand declaration.
- **"One host"** — rotate through three variants, each featuring one cofounder alone (Tarik, Ted, Hilmon). Ships only if each cofounder is comfortable being the solo face on a truck.
