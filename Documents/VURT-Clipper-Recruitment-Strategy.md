## The thesis

The clipper-program lane in vertical drama is empty at the top tier. ReelShort, DramaBox, ShortMax, NetShort, GoodShort run paid Meta/TikTok ads + brand-owned per-show TikTok playlists. None of them run an open Whop-style clipper bounty with public payouts. The only vertical-drama company doing it openly is DramaPops — a small/mid-tier app that launched their "Drama Lab" in March 2026, eight weeks ago.

VURT is not late to this lane — VURT is in the second wave. ReelShort can afford to skip it because their unit economics work on paid ads alone. VURT's economics need organic acceleration, which is exactly why this lane is ours to take.

The infrastructure to run it already exists (Whop, Discord, Clipping Culture, The Clip Ship). We don't have to build a marketplace — we plug into one.

## Compensation framework (rate card — Dioni sets the actual numbers)

Industry benchmarks as of 2026 from Whop/Vyro/N3on/Adin Ross campaigns:

| Vertical | Typical CPM | Source |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Crypto / finance | $4–$6 | Clippa.net 2025 guide |
| SaaS / B2B | $3–$5 | Clippa.net |
| Premium creator (MrBeast Vyro) | $3.00 | Vyro public docs |
| Whop standard | $0.50–$3.00 | Whop blog |
| Health / wellness | $2–$4 | Clippa.net |
| E-commerce / lifestyle | $1.50–$3 | Clippa.net |
| MrBeast original direct-pay (pre-Vyro) | $0.50 | Complex |
| N3on / Adin Ross streamer programs | $0.40–$0.50 | Tubefilter, Dexerto |

**Strategic landing zone:** $1.00–$2.00 CPM blended.

- Below $0.50 CPM → only spam farms show up.
- Above $2.00 CPM → competing with crypto auction and we lose.
- $1.00–$2.00 CPM matches Whop standard and aligns with AVOD economics.

### Recommended structure (3 tiers — Dioni decides actual dollars)

| Tier | Who | Base per approved clip | CPM | Per-post cap | Milestone bonuses |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Verified Drama Clippers** (Tier 1) | Approved roster, drama/booktok niche, US-skewed | Base $ | Mid-CPM $ | Cap $ | At 250K + 1M views |
| **Open Pool** (Tier 2) | Anyone passing Whop campaign rules + geo screen | $0 | Lower CPM $ | Smaller cap | Flat bonus at 1M |
| **Brand Ambassadors** (Tier 3) | Top 10 monthly performers, promoted from Tier 1 | Retainer + early access | Higher CPM $ | Highest cap | Monthly leaderboard prize |

### Realistic monthly spend bands (industry comps — Dioni sets actual)

- **Pilot** (Months 1–2): industry "meaningful test" floor is \~$2,000; established-brand pilot range $5K–$50K (Overlap.ai). Adin Ross-tier campaigns hit $1.4M over 5 weeks (N3on).
- **Scale** (Months 3+): Forbes/Bloomberg report contract-clipper campaigns typically $10K+/mo when working.

These are bands, not recommendations. Final number is Dioni's call based on what VURT can absorb.

### What KPI to actually pay against

**Verified platform-native views, with watch-percentage floor.** Specifically:

- TikTok / Reels / Shorts API view counts pulled 14 days post-publish (lets the clip stabilize, lets bot views get scrubbed)
- Only clips with ≥30% average watch-through count toward CPM (filters click-bait shock cuts)
- UTM-tagged clicks to myvurt.com tracked *separately* — for tier promotion and to identify revenue drivers, NOT as the payment trigger

We're AVOD. Pay metric should match revenue metric (impressions). Don't pay on clicks/signups.

## Anti-fraud guardrails (mandatory at launch)

 1. **Whop platform layer** — inherit their 24-hour payout buffer + post-approval bot sweep + KYC.
 2. **Geo gate** — 50%+ US audience verified by analytics screen-record, mandatory for Tier 1.
 3. **Minimum 5,000 verified views** before any CPM accrues. Filters volume spam.
 4. **On-screen myvurt.com or @myvurt for ≥2 seconds** — drives attribution + brand recall.
 5. **Per-clipper monthly cap** until trust history accrues (Tier 2 lower than Tier 1).
 6. **Captions burned-in required** — forces real editing labor, not auto-repost.
 7. **No bare reposts** of full VURT-cut social clips — clippers must add new edit value (different cut, different hook, different overlay).
 8. **3-day account warmup** before posting (clipper community standard — fresh accounts get TikTok-shadowbanned).
 9. **Submit-within-1-hour-of-posting rule** prevents late-attribution gaming.
10. **No-spoiler list per show** — never clip the finale beat.

## Why this is defensible (the strategic moat)

1. **First-mover in vertical drama** — ReelShort/DramaBox aren't doing this. DramaPops just started. The category-lock is available.
2. **Catalog control** — VURT owns its own IP (Swirl deals, originals, Glo Navy unscripted). Crazy Maple licenses from Zhangyue. We can permission clippers without external rights cleanup.
3. **AVOD alignment** — Views ARE the revenue event for VURT. Every other vertical-drama brand monetizes via paywall coin packages, which makes clipper attribution math messy. Ours is clean.
4. **The fan-edit pool already exists** — ReelShort/DramaBox actor stans are doing the work uncompensated. We can convert them to paid VURT clippers.
