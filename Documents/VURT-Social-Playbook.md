## The Foundation

### Content Format: 5-Clip Arc

Every title gets 5 clips released over time. Each clip serves a specific narrative function:

| Position | Name | Purpose |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Clip 1 | **Hook** | Stop the scroll. Create curiosity. Introduce the world. |
| Clip 2 | **Escalation** | Raise the stakes. Deepen the conflict or mystery. |
| Clip 3 | **Twist** | The moment that changes how you see everything. |
| Clip 4 | **Confrontation** | The clash. Characters face each other directly. |
| Clip 5 | **Edge of Resolution** | Leave them needing the next watch. |

**Rule:** Release clips one at a time. Let each one breathe before dropping the next.

---

### 72-Hour Evaluation

After each clip goes live, watch it for 72 hours. Classify it:

| Tier | Definition | Action |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Winner** (top 20%) | Views and shares above norm | Repost to other platforms. Make follow-up content. Boost with paid if budget allows. |
| **Solid** (middle 60%) | Steady performance | Keep in rotation. Move to next clip. |
| **Under** (bottom 20%) | Significantly below norm | Analyze why (hook? timing? platform?). Re-cut or shelve. |

Document the classification in the Content Tracker after 72 hours.

## Pre-Posting Checklist

### Editor Deliverables (Per Clip)

Before post day, the editor delivers:

- [ ]  Clean vertical MP4 (no watermark)

- [ ]  SRT subtitle file

- [ ]  Burned-in subtitles — TikTok style (2-4 words at a time, consistent font weight, white or cream text with subtle shadow or background bar)

- [ ]  Burned-in subtitles — Clean style (for IG, FB)

- [ ]  Naming convention: `{Title}_Clip_{Number}_Platform` (e.g., `file KarmaInHeels_Clip4_IG.mp4`)

**One clean export works for IG, FB, TikTok, and YT Shorts.** The SRT is the only extra step for YouTube.

## Posting Guide by Platform

### Instagram Reels

**Per clip:**

1. Add subtitles (clean style)
2. **Invite to Collaborate** — exec producer, director, top 2 actors (minimum). Meta allows 3 invites max — invite the top 3 by follower count from that group.
3. **Link a Reel** — connect to the best-performing previous clip from the same title. This keeps audiences watching series content back-to-back.
4. **Write caption and tag everyone** — every person involved (invited collaborators AND not-invited cast/crew) gets tagged in the caption body.
5. Post

**Immediately after posting — Stories:**

1. **Story 1 — Reshare the Reel.** Add caption overlay + Link sticker (myvurt.com or direct show URL). Example: *"She got caught and still made it his fault. Watch this."*
2. **Story 2 — Cast tags.** Black background or still frame. Tag everyone (exec producer, director, cast). No caption. They repost, their audiences see VURT.
3. **Story 3 — Poll or Question sticker.** Fit the question to the scene. Example: *"Would you go back for revenge?" YES / ABSOLUTELY*

*Note: Vary Story 3 format over time — sometimes a poll, sometimes a question, sometimes a "this or that." Keep it fresh.*

**Caption formula:**

```markdown
{One-liner from the scene that creates a hook}
{1-2 lines of context without giving it away}
{Title} — full series streaming free on VURT.
myvurt.com

#VURT #{TitleWithoutSpaces} #MicroDrama #{GenreHashtag} #IndieFilm
```

**Hashtags (all clips):** `#VURT #MicroDrama #IndieFilm`\
**Per title:** Create and use a title-specific hashtag (e.g., `#KarmaInHeels`)\
**Discovery:** `#DramaTok #WhatToWatch #NewOnVURT #FreeStreaming`

*Hashtags go at the end. Keep them relevant — don't spam.*

### Facebook Reels

**Do NOT auto-post from IG.** Post individually for more reach.

**Per clip:**

1. Upload the clean-style subtitled video directly to the VURT Facebook page
2. Write caption (same core message as IG, Facebook voice can be slightly more conversational)
3. **Drop the clickable link directly in the caption** — Facebook allows this. Use it. Point to myvurt.com or the direct show URL.
4. Share to Facebook Story

**Caption formula:**

```markdown
{Name of scene moment or key line}

{Context without spoilers}

Full episode — free on VURT. {direct link}

#VURT #{Title} #MicroDrama
```

**Story:** Same as IG Stories. Reshare the post to your Facebook Story with the Link sticker.

---

### TikTok

**Per clip:**

1. Add subtitles (TikTok style — 2-4 word pop, fast reads)
2. Tag the team in the caption: `@snooperstar` and any other confirmed TikTok handles from the cast/crew
3. Write caption (shorter than IG — TikTok moves fast)

**Caption formula:**

```markdown
{Key dialogue or one-liner that stops the scroll}

{One sentence of context. Don't give it away.}

Karma in Heels. Free on VURT. Link in bio.

#VURT #KarmaInHeels #MicroDrama #DramaTok #IndieFilm
```

**Every TikTok post — without exception:**

1. **Caption mention:** End with "Stream free at myvurt.com" or "myvurt.com" so they know where to go
2. **Pin a comment:** "Stream free at myvurt.com" — pin it to the top of the comments. Do this on every video until you hit 1,000 followers and get the clickable bio link.

---

### YouTube Shorts

**Per clip:**

1. Add subtitles (TikTok style works here too — 2-4 words at a time)
2. Get SRT from Frame.io
3. Upload to YouTube

**Upload settings:**

- **Title:** `{Title} | A VURT Original` (e.g., `Karma in Heels | A VURT Original`)
- **Description:** `A VURT Original. Streaming free at myvurt.com`
- **Video language:** English
- **Caption certification:** None
- **Category:** Entertainment
- **Visibility:** Public
- **Playlist:** Create a playlist per title on first upload (e.g., "Karma in Heels"). Add all future clips from that title to the same playlist.

**Caption formula (YouTube title is the caption — make it count):**

```markdown
{Title} | A VURT Original
```

## Premiere Day Checklist

When a new title officially premieres on VURT:

- [ ] **Morning — Premiere Day Story.** Simple "TODAY" or "IT'S HERE" story with key art. Builds anticipation before the main post.

- [ ] **Main post.** Drop Clip 1 (Hook) on all platforms simultaneously with premiere-day caption energy.

- [ ] **Cast repost request.** DM all cast/crew handles after tagging them. Ask them to reshare. Most will if you ask — it's free distribution.

- [ ] **Update bio links.** Change "Coming Soon" to the actual show URL on premiere day.

## Director Spotlight Campaign

**Purpose:** Build filmmaker recognition and surface VURT as a creator-first platform. Every original title gets one spotlight post before premiere.

**Prerequisites (confirmed before posting):**
- 5 clips (or clip count confirmed)
- Key art / poster
- Director headshot
- 1-2 sentence filmmaker quote
- Cast list with roles and IG handles
- Direct show URL (myvurt.com/{slug})

---

**Platforms:** IG Feed + FB Feed + LinkedIn + IG Stories.

**Caption formula:**

```markdown
VURT Filmmakers Spotlight: {Director Name}

{Director and writer of {Title}. Premiering {Date}, free on VURT.}

Every filmmaker behind our originals gets their moment — regardless of whether they're exclusive to VURT. Independent creators deserve the platform.

myvurt.com

#{TitleWithoutSpaces} #FilmmakerSpotlight #VURT #IndieFilm
```

**Stories:** Reshare the feed post to Stories. Add Link sticker pointing to myvurt.com or the direct show URL.

**Link strategy:** On premiere day, update your IG and TikTok bio links from "Coming Soon" to the actual show page URL (e.g. `myvurt.com/the-parking-lot`). Every day that link sits on "Coming Soon" is a missed conversion.

**Tag everyone in the post caption:**
- Exec producer
- Director
- Top 2 actors
- Minimum 4 tags per post. More if handles exist.

## Caption Voice Rules

1. **Never give away the twist.** Hook without spoiling.
2. **End every caption with a destination** — "free on VURT," "myvurt.com," or "link in bio."
3. **Hashtags are functional, not performative** — only use tags that actually help discovery.
4. **No cursing in captions** unless the scene dialogue is the hook and it's authentic to the moment.
5. **Tag the team** — every caption should tag at minimum the exec producer, director, and top 2 actors if IG handles exist.
6. **Platform voice:** TikTok is fastest/boldest. IG is slightly more polished. Facebook is conversational. YouTube title IS the caption — keep it clean.

## What to Never Do

- ❌ Auto-post IG to Facebook (post individually)
- ❌ Tag people not in the clip unless they made the movie
- ❌ Give Tubi credit (VURT originals are VURT originals)
- ❌ Use `#BlackCinema` as a default tag on every post (save for culturally specific campaigns)
- ❌ Post a clip without subtitles
- ❌ Skip the pinned comment on TikTok
- ❌ Skip Stories after posting a Reel
- ❌ Post multiple clips from the same title on the same day (let each one breathe)
