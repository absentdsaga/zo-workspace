THE 5-STEP TEST FLOW

01 — Wallet Connect — Genesis SBT verification. Device ownership confirmed. Anonymous but verifiable.

02 — First Impressions — Load time. Did it crash? Did they understand what the app is?

03 — Task Completion — Can they actually do what your app is for? Where do they get stuck?

04 — Deep Feedback — 150+ character written feedback. Screenshots. What would make this better?

05 — Rating + Review — Public dApp Store review. This is the output that matters most.
