## Caption Framework

Every post follows the VURT voice: direct, clean, confident. No hype. No cringe.

### Clip Bait Captions
- "She wasn't supposed to find out like this."
- "He thought nobody was watching."
- "This is the moment everything changed."
- [Scene-specific one-liner that creates curiosity gap]
- Always end with: "Full episode on VURT. Link in bio."

### Hashtag Strategy
**Core (every post):** #VURT #MicroDrama #VerticalCinema
**Genre-specific:** #TrueCrime #ThrillerTok #DramaTok #FaithFilm #BlackCinema #IndieFilm
**Title-specific:** #KillerStepdad #MiamiKingpins #FatalLust (create the hashtag for every title)
**Discovery:** #WhatToWatch #NewOnVURT #StreamingNow

### Platform Adjustments
- **TikTok:** Shorter captions. Trending hashtags. Hook in text overlay.
- **IG:** Longer captions OK. Hashtags at end. CTA for saves.
- **YT Shorts:** Title is the caption. Keywords matter for search.
- **X:** Commentary voice. Quote-tweet energy. No hashtag spam.
