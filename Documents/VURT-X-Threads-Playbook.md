# VURT X + Threads Playbook

**Version 1.2 | April 20, 2026**\
*A living document — update after every posting sprint*

---

## Platform Roles

| Platform | Role for VURT |
| --- | --- |
| **X** | Conversation + press layer. Engage journalists, entertainment writers, industry accounts. React to cultural moments in real time. Build authority. |
| **Threads** | Growth + community layer. Newer algo tailwind, Meta ecosystem cross-surface with IG. Build VURT's voice with text and lightweight media. |

**They are NOT:** clip-distribution platforms. TikTok/IG/YT carry clips. X and Threads carry the voice.

---

## The VURT Voice on Text Platforms

What VURT organizes around — the actual territory:

- **Vertical storytelling** — the format, where microdrama is going, what competitors get wrong
- **Indie filmmakers** — championing people the industry ignores. "Submit your film. Live in 48 hours. You keep your IP."
- **Culture** — lived, multi-genre, not performed
- **The streaming industry** — Quibi lessons, Disney/Peacock vertical pivots, the whole wave
- **Stories on the platform** — drama, horror, comedy, romance — all of it

**Rules:**
- No hype words. No influencer excitement.
- Short sentences. Clear intent. No fluff.
- Have a POV. Brand accounts without voice are dead space on both platforms.

## Content Pillars

Rotate through these across the week. Each post should fit into one:

### 1. Clip-Adjacent (25%)
Pull the punchiest line of dialogue from a clip and post it as text. Reply to your own post with the video.
> "She asked for 20 minutes. He said he didn't have the time."
>
> 35 & Ticking. Free on VURT.

### 2. Industry Takes (20%)
VURT's POV on the streaming and microdrama industry.
> Disney just launched "Verts." Peacock is doing vertical NBA broadcasts. Netflix is redesigning for short-form.
>
> Every major streamer is chasing vertical. None of them are built for indie filmmakers.
>
> We are.

### 3. Filmmaker Spotlights (15%)
Champion the people on the platform. Name them.
> Racheal Leigh shot Bride To Be out of Launceston, Tasmania. Produced it. Directed it. Edited it.
>
> Premieres Tuesday. Free on VURT.

### 4. Founder Stories (15%)
Tell the team's stories from the brand account in third person. Mark's Quibi run. Ted's Slip-N-Slide legacy. The team's pedigree.
> One of our co-founders worked at Quibi. He watched what killed it up close.
>
> 1. No screenshots or sharing allowed.
> 2. No social layer.
> 3. $1.75B spent on production, zero spent on distribution.
>
> VURT is the opposite on all three.

### 5. Community / Reply Bait (15%)
Ask questions that pull conversation.
> What's the last indie film that stuck with you?
> Drop the title.

### 6. Culture Commentary (10%)
Connect to something happening in culture that week. No forced tie-ins.

## Cadence

Baseline (reality check at current staffing — you + intern, brand account only):

| Platform | VURT Brand | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **X** | 3-5/day | Mix of original posts, quote-tweets, replies |
| **Threads** | 3-5/day | Heavier on replies — algo rewards it |

**Scale trigger:** When you add a dedicated social writer, push brand to 8-12/day on each. Threads rewards consistency at volume.

**Phase 2 trigger:** Once brand voice is locked and the 2-week test proves traction, layer in personal accounts (Mark/Ted/Dioni at 1-2/day each).

**Do not:**
- Auto-crosspost IG → Threads (algo tanks it)
- Copy/paste X posts to Threads (they index differently)
- Post the same text on both accounts in the same hour

**Do:**
- Repurpose same idea with different framing across both
- Stagger by 2-4 hours if using similar content
- Reply to other accounts. Both platforms reward replies heavily.

## Format Rules

### X
- Text posts: 1-2 sentences max for highest engagement
- Threads (the Twitter feature, lowercase t): 3-7 posts, each adds to the story
- Quote-retweet press coverage with a founder take on top
- Attach images/video to ~40% of posts
- Hashtags: 0-1 max. They don't help on X.

### Threads
- Text posts: Can run longer (2-4 sentences), Threads rewards substance
- Images + text performs better than text-only (Meta algo loves image)
- Vertical video clips work well (cross-surface with IG)
- Hashtags: 0-1 max. Topics > hashtags on Threads.
- Reply chains are surfaced heavily — engage every comment

## Engagement (More Important Than Posting)

Both platforms rank reply activity above follower count for distribution.

**Daily engagement targets (intern or you):**
- 10-15 replies to culture/industry/filmmaker accounts on X
- 10-15 replies on Threads
- Quote-tweet 2-3 press mentions or relevant industry news per day

**Accounts to follow + engage:**

| Category | Who |
| --- | --- |
| Trade press | @Variety, @THR, @TechCrunch, @Deadline, @IndieWire |

| Streaming industry | @netflix, @hulu, @peacock, @StreamingWars |

## Links & CTAs

### X
- Clickable links in posts hurt reach slightly — X deprioritizes external links
- Workaround: Drop the link in a reply to your own post, not the main post
- For VURT titles: `myvurt.com` or `myvurt.com/detail/micro_series/{slug}`

### Threads
- Links are fully clickable and don't tank reach as hard
- Can drop `myvurt.com/detail/micro_series/{slug}` directly in the post
- Link stickers not available on Threads (no Stories layer)

## Metrics to Track

Weekly in the Content Tracker:

| Metric | Why |
| --- | --- |
| Follower growth (VURT brand account, both platforms) | Foundation metric |
| Replies per post | Algo ranks this highest |
| Profile visits | Intent signal — people want more |
| Link clicks to myvurt.com | Conversion signal |
| Repost/quote count | Organic reach amplification |
| Top-performing post each week | Learn what voice is working |

**72-hour classification** — same as other platforms:
- Winner (top 20%) → repurpose, expand, make it a thread/series
- Solid (middle 60%) → keep in rotation
- Under (bottom 20%) → kill the format, try something else

## What NOT to Do

- ❌ Auto-crosspost from IG or TikTok (dead giveaway, both algos tank it)
- ❌ Post the same text on X and Threads same hour
- ❌ Corporate-sounding announcements without voice
- ❌ Hashtag stacks (0-1 hashtag max on both)
- ❌ Ignore replies on posts that get traction
- ❌ Post clips as the main content (the clips live on TikTok/IG/YT — these platforms are for voice)
- ❌ Mention competitors by name negatively (Tubi, ReelShort) — position against the category, not accounts
- ❌ Use "Black cinema" as VURT's lane — VURT is multi-genre, culture-first
